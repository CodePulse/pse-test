Overview of examples

A range of examples is included with gPROMS ProcessBuilder. The example projects can be accessed from the toolbar by pressing the Open an example project button. All examples require the gML Multipack 1: Basics, Signal, Control, Flow Transportation, Heat Exchange license. Some examples require additional licences.

The broad range of included examples can be divided in the following categories:

Category Description
Flow transportation The examples in this category illustrate the use of models from the gML Flow Transportation library, like compressors, pumps, valves and pipes.
Flowsheeting The examples in this category demonstrate the general modelling capabilities of gPROMS ProcessBuilder and how they can be applied to simulate different chemical engineering processes, such as CO2 compression, separation of hydrocarbon mixtures and production of styrene.
Industrial cases The examples in this category illustrate the use of gPROMS ProcessBuilder in the area of industrial gases.
Natural gas The examples in this category demonstrate the use of gPROMS ProcessBuilder in modelling processes in the natural gas chain.
Reaction The examples in this category illustrate the use of models from the Reaction library.
Separation The examples in this category illustrate the use of models from the gML Separations Fluid-Fluid and gML Separations Adsorption libraries. These libraries contain models like flash drums, distillation columns and adsorption beds.
Signals and control Examples in this category illustrate the additional modelling flexibility provided by the gML Signal library and how models in this library can be linked to unit equipment models from other libraries.
Custom modelling The examples in this category illustrate some of the custom modelling capabilities available in gPROMS ProcessBuilder.
