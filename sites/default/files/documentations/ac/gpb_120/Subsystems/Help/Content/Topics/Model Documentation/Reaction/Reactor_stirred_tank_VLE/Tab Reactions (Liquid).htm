Reactions (Liquid)

The Reactions (Liquid) tab is used to specify the reactions taking place in the liquid phase.

Name Default Unit Description
Reactions specification Selector
Specified here
The reactions are specified in this unit.
From kinetic port connection
Reaction informations are obtained from the kinetic port.
Using custom kinetic models
User defined custom kinetic model.
Kinetic model Selector
Power law
Reaction rate basis Selector
per volume (kmol/h/m3)
Reaction rates are specified on a volumetric basis.
per mass of catalyst (kmol/h/kg)
Reaction rates are specified per mass of catalyst (heterogeneous reactions).
per surface of catalyst (kmol/h/m2)
Reaction rates are specified per surface area of catalyst (heterogeneous reactions).

Molar fractions

Mass concentrations

Custom kinetic model Selector Standard custom kinetics
Irreversible reactions - Enter the name of all the irreversible reactions taking place. These names are used to populate the tables of the dialog.
Reversible reactions - Enter the name of all the reversible reactions taking place. These names are used to populate the tables of the dialog.
Equilibrium reactions - Enter the name of all the equilibrium reactions taking place. These names are used to populate the tables of the dialog.
Stoichiometric matrix - Stoichiometric coefficients of the reactions defined above. A negative integer value indicates the component is consumed by a reaction, a positive value indicates that the component is produced by the reaction and a zero value indicates that the component does not participate in the reaction.
