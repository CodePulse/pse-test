Main

The Main tab is used to set the energy split fractions or alternatively the energy rates for the Splitter energy outlets.

Name Default Unit Description
Specification type Selector
Split fraction
Use a split fraction to specify how much energy goes to each output.
Pressure-driven (flow-driven initialisation)
Specify the output energy rates directly.
Energy split fractions - Sets the energy split fractions for the outlet streams.
Energy rate outlet kW Sets the energy rates for the outlet streams.
