Performance maps

To predict compressor outlet pressure based on the flowrate and vice-versa the use of performance maps (or "head and efficiency curves") is required. These maps provide a relationship between the volumetric flowrate though the compressor and the polytropic head and efficiency. Both 1D and 2D maps can be used. 1D maps provide a head and efficiency relationship at fixed design compressor speed. In this case, affinity laws are used to predict off-design (speed) performance. Optionally, the 1D map can be specified on a non-dimensional basis. 2D maps provide a relationship for head and efficiency at all feasible compressor speeds.
