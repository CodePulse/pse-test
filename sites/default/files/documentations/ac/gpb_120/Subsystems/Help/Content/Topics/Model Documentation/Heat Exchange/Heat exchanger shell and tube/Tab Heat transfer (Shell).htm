Heat transfer (Shell)

The Heat transfer (Shell) tab is used to specify data relative to the shell side heat transfer.

Name Default Unit Description
Shell fluid to tube wall heat transfer coefficient Selector
Specified
Specifies the heat transfer coefficient directly.
Calculated
Calculates the heat transfer coefficient from a given correlation.
Shell fluid to shell wall heat transfer coefficient Selector
Specified
Specifies the heat transfer coefficient directly.
Calculated
Calculates the heat transfer coefficient from a given correlation.
Fouling effect specification Selector
Off
The fouling effect is not taken into account.
On
The fouling effect is taken into account.
Fouling factors specification Selector
Specified
Specifies the fouling factor directly.
Calculated
Calculates the fouling factor from a given correlation.
Fouling factors correlation Selector
Custom
User-supplied equations listed in the htc_fouling_1D_custom_gML model are used to determine the fouling factor.
Heat conduction in the shell wall Selector
Off
The axial heat conduction term in the energy balance is not taken into account.
On
The axial heat conduction in the wall is calculated based on the thermal conductivity of the wall material.
Shell fluid to tube wall heat transfer coefficient correlation Selector
Power-law
Calculates the heat transfer coefficient from a power-law correlation.
Bell-Delaware
Calculates the heat transfer coefficient from the Bell-Delaware method (Shah & Sekulic, 2003, p. 482)1Shah, R.K. & Sekulic, D.P., 2003. Fundamentals of Heat Exchanger Design, John Wiley & Sons, Inc..
Custom
User-supplied equations listed in the htc_channel_1D_custom_gML model are given to determine the heat transfer coefficient.
Shell fluid to shell wall heat transfer coefficient correlation Selector
Power-law
Calculates the heat transfer coefficient from a power-law correlation.
Bell-Delaware
Calculates the heat transfer coefficient from the Bell-Delaware method (Shah & Sekulic, 2003, p. 482)2Shah, R.K. & Sekulic, D.P., 2003. Fundamentals of Heat Exchanger Design, John Wiley & Sons, Inc..
Custom
User-supplied equations listed in the htc_channel_1D_custom_gML model are given to determine the heat transfer coefficient.
Shell fluid to tube wall heat transfer coefficient W m-2 K-1 Heat transfer coefficient between the shell and the tube wall.
Shell fluid to shell wall heat transfer coefficient W m-2 K-1 Heat transfer coefficient between the shell and the shell wall.
Design flowrate kg/s Design flowrate used in the Power-law correlation.
Design heat transfer coefficient W m-2 K-1 Heat-transfer coefficient at the design flowrate (for the Power-law correlation).
Flow correction factor - Power-law correlation flow correction factor.
Shell wall thermal conductivity W m-1 K-1 Thermal conductivity of the tube wall.
Shell side fouling factor (Tube bundle) K kW-1 m-2 Shell side fouling factor to the tubes.
Shell side fouling factor (Shell wall) K kW-1 m-2 Shell side fouling factor to the shell wall.
