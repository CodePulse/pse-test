Operation

The Operation tab is used together with the Design tab to specify performance data. This tab is not available if the performance mode is turned off.

Name Default Unit Description
Pump speed Selector
Specified
Internal pump speed specification.
From connection
The speed of the pump is specified by an external model (e.g. a Shaft model).
Perfect control (no dynamics)
The pump speed is defined implicitly by assigning a connected variable (e.g. outlet pressure).
Pressure specification Selector
Pressure outlet
Fluid pressure at the unit outlet.
Pressure ratio
Pressure ratio between the outlet and the inlet fluid.
Pressure increase
Pressure increase from the inlet fluid to the outlet fluid.
Power
The outlet pressure is implicitly defined by the power supplied, which can be specified directly or obtained by the gMLEnergy port.
Pump speed Hz Pump speed.
Pressure outlet bar Pressure at the pump outlet.
Pressure ratio - Pressure ratio between the outlet and inlet fluid.
Pressure increase bar Pressure increase from the inlet fluid to the outlet fluid.
Total power kW Total power supplied to the pump (some power might be dissipated depending on the mechanical efficiency).
