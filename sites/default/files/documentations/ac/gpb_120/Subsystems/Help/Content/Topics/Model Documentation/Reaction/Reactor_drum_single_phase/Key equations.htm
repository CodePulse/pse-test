Component concentration basis

When a kinetic mechanism or set of equilibrium reactions is specified, the component concentration basis can be changed to use different measures for the amount of a component. The units of measurement for each basis are given below.

  Molar concentration of component i mol/m3
  Mass fraction of component i kg/kg
  Molar fraction of component i mol/mol
  Partial pressure of component i bar
  Activity of component i -
  Fugacity of component i bar

The choice of a certain basis will influence the unit of the reaction constant for that reaction. From this point onwards the concentration of a component expressed in the chosen basis will be referred to as:

Concentration of component i expressed in the chosen basis. Units depend on the basis
