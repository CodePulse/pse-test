gMLMaterial mass balance to gMLMaterial

The gMLMaterial mass balance to gMLMaterial model can be used to convert from a stream carrying only information on mass flowrate, composition and pressure to a stream carrying information on mass flowrate, composition, pressure and mass specific enthalpy. This is useful if a flowsheet is to include sections which take into account the energy balance (and therefore require the use of physical properties) as well as sections which do not take this into account.

As the gMLMaterial mass balance stream on the inlet of this model does not include information on the mass specific enthalpy, either temperature or enthalpy needs to be specified in the converter. This temperature or enthalpy is then used as an inlet boundary condition for the downstream section of the flowsheet which does take into account the energy balance.
