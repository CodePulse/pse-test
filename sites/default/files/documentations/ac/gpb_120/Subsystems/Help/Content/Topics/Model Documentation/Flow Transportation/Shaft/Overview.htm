Shaft

The Shaft model represents a shaft to which rotating equipment can be connected. It ensures that all the connected rotating equipment operates at the same frequency and aggregates the torque contributions of each piece of equipment. For steady-state simulations, the Shaft model ensures the overall torque balance is zero. In dynamic mode it predicts the drive rotational speed from the total torque balance and the sum of the inertia of the shaft and the various pieces of rotating equipment connected to it.
