Pressure

The Pressure tab is used to specify the pressure along the column.

Name Default Unit Description
Pressure profile specification Selector
Pressure uniform over the column
Pressure is the same on all trays.
Linear profile, specify top and bottom pressure
Pressure varies linearly between the top and the bottom of the column. Top and bottom pressures in the column are specified.
Linear profile, specify the pressure drop per stage
Pressure varies linearly between the top and the bottom of the column. Constant pressure drop per stage and one extra pressure on the column are specified.
Calculated from column hydraulics
Determines the pressure on each stage based on hydraulic correlations.

Pressure

 Selector
Specified here (Pressure uniform over column)
Uses the pressure specified in the dialog.
From feed
Uses the pressure of the selected feed and applies it uniformly over the column.
Top and bottom pressures specified here (Linear profile, specify top and bottom pressure)
The two different pressures used for the linear profile are located at the top and the bottom of the column and are specified here.
Top pressure from feed (at location) and bottom pressure specified here
The two different pressures used for the linear profile are located at the same location as one of the feed for the top one and the bottom of the column for the bottom one. The bottom pressure is specified here.
Top pressure specified here and bottom pressure from feed (at location)
The two different pressures used for the linear profile are located at the top of the column for the top one and at the same location as one of the feed for the bottom one. The top pressure is specified here.

Pressure drop calculation Selector
Simple Bernoulli
Determines the pressure drop per stage via a flow-pressure relationship in the form or the Bernoulli equation.
Advanced correlations
Uses advanced correlations to determine the pressure drop, defined as the sum of the dry and aerated liquid pressure drops.
Pressure drop mode Selector
Design
This selector is available when using the simple Bernoulli option for the pressure drop calculation. In design mode, the adjustable coefficient of the correlation is calculated automatically.
Performance
This selector is available when using the simple Bernoulli option for the pressure drop calculation. In performance mode, the adjustable coefficient of the correlation must be supplied in the specification window.
