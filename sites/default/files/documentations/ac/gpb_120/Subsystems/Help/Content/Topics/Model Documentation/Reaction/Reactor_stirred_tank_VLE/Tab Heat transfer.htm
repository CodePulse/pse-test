Heat transfer

The Heat transfer tab is used to calculate the heat transferred between the reactor and the wall.

Name Default Unit Description
Reactor to wall heat transfer coefficient Selector
Specify
Specifies the reactor to wall heat transfer coefficient directly.
Custom
User-defined correlation to calculate the reactor to wall heat transfer coefficient.
Reactor to wall heat transfer coefficient W m-2 K-1
Value of the heat transfer coefficient for the heat transfer from the fluid in the reactor to the wall.
