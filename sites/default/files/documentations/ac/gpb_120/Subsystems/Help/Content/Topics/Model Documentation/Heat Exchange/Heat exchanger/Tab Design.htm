Pure component condensation or evaporation in heat exchanger Selector

No pure component condensation or evaporation in heat exchanger. Normal NTU relations assuming constant heat capacity will be used.
Hot side
Pure component condensation or evaporation in heat exchanger for the hot side steam. NTU relations for are modified to assume no temperature change on the hot side instead of constant heat capacity on this side.

Pure component condensation or evaporation in heat exchanger for the hot side steam. NTU relations for are modified to assume no temperature change on the cold side instead of constant heat capacity on this side.
Both sides
Pure component condensation or evaporation in heat exchanger for both the hot and the cold side. NTU relations for are modified to assume no temperature change on both the hot and the cold side.
Maximal heat exchange limit calculation Selector
Determined from enthalpies
The maximal possible heat that can be exchanged is calculated from the enthalpy differences for each stream between its inlet temperature and the inlet temperature of the other stream.
Determined from inlet heat capacity and temperatures
The maximal possible heat that can be exchanged is calculated using the heat capacity of each stream and the temperature difference between the inlet temperature of the hot side and the cold side. Using this option may result in efficiencies that exceed 100 percent in case the constant heat capacity assumption is not accurate.
Determined from temperature difference ratios
The efficiency of the heat exchanger is related directly to the ratio between the temperature change of each stream and the difference between the inlet temperatures of the streams.
Multipass flow configuration (LMTD) Selector
1 shell pass / 2 tube passes
One fluid pass on the shell-side and two fluid passes on the tube-side.
1 shell pass / 4 tube passes
One fluid pass on the shell-side and four fluid passes on the tube-side.
1 shell pass / 6 tube passes
One fluid pass on the shell-side and six fluid passes on the tube-side.
1 shell pass / 8 tube passes
One fluid pass on the shell-side and eight fluid passes on the tube-side.
N shell passes / 2N tube passes
N fluid passes on the shell-side and 2N fluid passes on the tube-side. It is assumed that the number of transfer units is constant in each pass and equal to the total number of transfer units divided by the number of shell passes.
Multipass flow configuration (NTU) Selector
1 shell pass / 2, 4, ... tube passes
Configuration with one shell pass and an even number of tube passes.
N shell passes / 2N, 4N, ... tube passes
Configuration with N shell passes and an even number of tube passes.
Design options Selector
Specify U and extra outlet specification
The overall heat transfer coefficient and an extra outlet design specification are to be specified by the user (e.g. hot/cold fluid outlet temperature).
Specify A and extra outlet specification
The area used for heat transfer and an extra outlet design specification are to be specified by the user (e.g. hot/cold fluid outlet temperature).
