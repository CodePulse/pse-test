Tank

The Tank model is used to model a vessel for storage of liquids. The liquid in the tank is at a defined pressure, which may be for example the ambient pressure for an open tank or the regulated pressure for closed vessel. The liquid in the tank is assumed not to exchange mass or energy with the vapour above the liquid.

In steady-state mode, the outlet flowrate and composition are equal to the inlet flowrate and composition. In dynamic mode, changes in the liquid holdup and composition are determined from the inlet and outlet stream flowrates and compositions.
