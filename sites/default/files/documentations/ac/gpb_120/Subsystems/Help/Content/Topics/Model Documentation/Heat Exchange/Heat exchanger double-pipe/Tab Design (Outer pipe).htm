Design (Outer pipe)

The Design (Outer pipe) tab is used to specify data relating to the shape and dimensions of the outer pipe.

Name Default Unit Description
Outer pipe radius specification Selector
Diameter
The inner diameter of the outer pipe is specified.
Radius
The inner radius of the outer pipe is specified.
Outer wall specification Selector
Wall thickness
Determines the outer radius from the wall thickness.
Outer diameter
Determines the outer radius from its diameter.
Outer radius
Specifies the outer radius directly.
Outer area
Determines the outer radius by specifying the outer area of the wall.
Outer wall area specification Selector
Equal to outer wall outer area
Selects the outer wall area to be calculated from the hollow cylinder geometry and the wall dimensions provided.
Specify
The outer wall area can be set. This option is useful if the outer tube has fins, since the total heat transfer area can be easily specified.
