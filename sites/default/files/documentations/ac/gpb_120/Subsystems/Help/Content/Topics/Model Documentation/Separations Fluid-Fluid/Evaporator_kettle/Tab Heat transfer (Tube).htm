Heat transfer (Tube)

The Heat transfer (Tube) tab is used to assign or calculate tube heat transfer coefficients.

Name Default Unit Description
Tube fluid to tube wall heat transfer coefficient Selector
Specify
Specifies the tube fluid to tube wall heat transfer coefficient directly.
Custom
User-specified correlation to calculate the tube fluid to tube wall heat transfer coefficient.
Heat conduction in the wall Selector
Off
The heat conduction term in the energy balance is ignored.
On
The energy balance includes the heat conduction term which varies with the tube wall thermal conductivity.
Heat transfer constant correlation Selector
Power-law
Calculates the heat transfer coefficient from a power-law correlation.
Tube
Calculates the heat transfer coefficient from a turbulent flow correlation (Shah & Sekulic, 2003, p. 482)1Shah, R.K. & Sekulic, D.P., 2003. Fundamentals of Heat Exchanger Design, John Wiley & Sons, Inc..
Custom
User-supplied equations listed in the htc_channel_1D_custom_gML model are given to determine the heat transfer coefficient.
Design flowrate kg/s Power-law correlation design flowrate.
Design heat transfer coefficient W m-2 K-1 Power-law correlation design heat transfer coefficient.
Flow correction factor - Power-law correlation flow correction factor.
