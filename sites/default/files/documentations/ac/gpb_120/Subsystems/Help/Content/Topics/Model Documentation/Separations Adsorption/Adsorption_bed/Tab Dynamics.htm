Dynamics

The Dynamics tab is used to specify the initial conditions of the bed at the start of a dynamic simulation.

Name Default Unit Description
Composition basis Selector
Mass
Specify the initial composition in the bed on a mass basis.
Molar
Specify the initial composition in the bed on a molar basis.

Product side initial pressure

bar

Initial pressure at the product side. The pressure profile in the bed is a linear interpolation between the product and feed side pressures.

Feed side initial pressure

 bar
Initial pressure at the feed side. The pressure profile in the bed is a linear interpolation between the product and feed side pressures.

Initial mass fraction

 kg/kg

Mass fraction of the mixture that is initially in the bed.

Initial molar fraction mol/mol Molar fraction that is initially in the bed.
Initial temperature K Initial temperature in the bed.
