Temporary suspension of Project cross-referencing

At any point during a gPROMS ProcessBuilder session, the user may instruct gPROMS ProcessBuilder not to search other Projects for any missing Entities until further notice. This can be done by checking the Disable cross-references check-box in the cross-references tab of the Project's Properties dialog.
