Projects and the project tree

The project tree allows the user to navigate all the (Library) Projects and Cases that have been respectively opened and created during a gPROMS ProcessBuilder session. They are distinguished by their colours: yellow (Projects); blue(Library projects); and turquoise (Cases) and their ordering: Library projects, Projects and Cases. Projects of the same type are then ordered alphabetically.
