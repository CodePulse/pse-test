MSMPR crystallizer

This model describes a mixed suspension mixed product removal (MSMPR) crystallizer.  The scope of the model includes:

  • Cooling, flash cooling, evaporation, anti-solvent addition and reaction crystallization.
  • Batch, fed-batch and continuous operation
  • Crystallization mechanisms such as primary nucleation, secondary nucleation (as a result of attrition), growth (mass transfer and surface integration) and agglomeration
  • Liquid phase reactions
