PFR two phase liquid

The plug flow reactor two-liquid phase model consists of a 1D dynamic liquid phase tubular reactor model capable of describing both reaction and mass transfer phenomena in single- or two-liquid phase systems. The model supports multiple material inlets and one single material outlet.

Key features:

  • Continuous operation.
  • Transient plug flow description of transport of material through the unit.
  • Dispersive transport is considered.
  • Liquid phase reactions: The reaction scheme and reaction information (stoichiometry, reaction orders and kinetic parameters) are specified by the user.
  • Liquid phase reactions: Different rate expressions may be selected for each defined reaction.
  • Liquid-liquid mass transfer: partition coefficients and overall volumetric mass transfer coefficients (kLa) are specified by the user.
