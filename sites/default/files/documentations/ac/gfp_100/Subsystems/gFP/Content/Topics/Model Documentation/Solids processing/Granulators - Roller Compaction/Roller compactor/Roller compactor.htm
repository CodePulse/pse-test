Roller compactor

The roller compactor model is used to model a compaction process in which a stream of powder is fed between two parallel rotating cylinders.

Key features:

  • No accumulation of material within unit.
  • Extent of particle compaction is dependent on specifications.
  • The product consists of a solid sheet with a thickness equal to the roll gap.
