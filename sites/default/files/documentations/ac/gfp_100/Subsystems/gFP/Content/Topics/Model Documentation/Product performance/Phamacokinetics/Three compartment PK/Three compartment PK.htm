Three compartment PK

The three compartment PK model is used to characterise the pharmacokinetics in the body which it approximates as three compartments, and describes the plasma concentration of an externally administered drug substance over a period of time.

Key features:

  • Three compartments
  • First order or Michaelis Menten elimination kinetics
  • First pass effect
  • First order distribution kinetics
