General advice

General advice from Saltelli et al. (2008) on how to avoid well-known pitfalls, is paraphrased below:

  • Clearly identify the goal of the analysis
  • Refrain from analysing too many responses at once.

Failing to do either will return meaningless data. In addition, for uncertainty and sensitivity analysis, care should be taken when:

  • Specifying factors, their distribution and sampling restrictions
  • Interpreting results and understanding their limitations.

This documentation provides the framework to avoid these pitfalls.
