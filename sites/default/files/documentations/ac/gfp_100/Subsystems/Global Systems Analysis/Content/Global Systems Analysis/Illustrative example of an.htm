Illustrative example of an uncertainty analysis

In this example, GSA is used to understand how uncertainty in reaction kinetics affects the production of methyl acetate in a CSTR reactor.

An uncertainty analysis is performed with focus on the key chemical reaction of the process. The factors are the reaction activation energy and reaction pre-exponential coefficient. Both factors follow a normal distribution, of which the mean and standard deviation were obtained from a parameter estimation. The responses of interest are the yield, temperature and molar concentration. The uncertainty in the pre-exponential coefficient is plotted against the uncertainty in the yield.

A high level description of the case study can be found in 'Description of the illustrative example system'. Instructions to reproduce these results can be found in 'Case 3: Parameter uncertainty effect on KPIs'.
