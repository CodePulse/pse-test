Assumptions

The model is developed under the following assumptions:

  • The contents of stomach are uniform in space. (i.e. the vessel is well mixed)
  • The liquid and solid phases in the bulk are in thermal and mechanical equilibrium. (i.e. same temperature [37°C] and pressure [1 atm])
  • The liquid properties of outlet flows are equal to the properties of the liquid phase in the bulk of stomach.
