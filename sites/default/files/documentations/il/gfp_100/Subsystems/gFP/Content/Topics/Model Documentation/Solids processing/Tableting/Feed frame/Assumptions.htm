Assumptions

The model is developed under the following assumptions:

  • No compression of material to form tablets takes place – the unit describes the motion and concentration of phases and materials in the blend at the end of the transport process.
  • Inlet streams are perfectly mixed when entering the unit.
  • No energy transfer between phases and/or to the environment.
  • Transport of the material is governed by a plug flow description with dispersion in the axial direction.
  • No dispersion in the radial direction.
  • Size of the particulate phases does not impact the transport of flow.
  • Outflow of material is controlled by the operation of a tablet press (based on rotational speed and die fill capacity).
