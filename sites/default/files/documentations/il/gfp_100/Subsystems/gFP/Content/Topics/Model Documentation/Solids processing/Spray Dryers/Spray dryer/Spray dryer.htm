Spray dryer

This model is used to model the drying of droplets in a co-current spray dryer. The model assumes that a mixture of liquid and suspended solid is fed to the unit where it is atomised. The droplets produced then undergo drying as they fall through the unit. The model considers the change in the droplet size and temperature distributions due to drying.

The key features of this model are:

  • Both constant-rate and falling-rate drying regimes are considered.
  • The evolution of the size, composition and temperature distributions of the droplets is considered
