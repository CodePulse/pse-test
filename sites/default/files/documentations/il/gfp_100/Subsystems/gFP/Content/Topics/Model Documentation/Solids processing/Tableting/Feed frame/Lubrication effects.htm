Lubrication

Lubrication refers the addition of lubricants during powder mixing/blending operations. Lubrication helps to adjust product (tablet/capsule) quality attributes including tensile strength, disintegration and dissolution times etc. The extent of lubrication refers to amount of shear strain on powders (containing lubricant) which acts to disperse the lubricant over the available surface area of the powder.
