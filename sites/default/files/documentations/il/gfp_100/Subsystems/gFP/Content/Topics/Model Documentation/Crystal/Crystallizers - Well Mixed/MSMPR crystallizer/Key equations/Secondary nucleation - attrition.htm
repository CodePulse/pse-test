Secondary nucleation - attrition

There are various secondary nucleation mechanisms such as attrition, surface breeding, dendritic breeding, etc.  In gCRYSTAL, we consider two types, attrition and activated, which are described in 'Secondary nucleation - activated'. Secondary nucleation as a result of attrition of crystals tends to be the dominant secondary nucleation mechanism in industrial crystallization processes. Five model options are available for secondary nucleation (attrition) rate expressions in the Crystallizer_MSMPR model:

  1. Inactive
  2. Evans kinetics
  3. Gahn and Mersmann kinetics
  4. Power law kinetics
  5. Custom kinetics
