Contraction coefficient (α in Eq. (7) )

When gas enters the cyclone through the tangential inlet, it is forced towards the wall by the pressure field of the vortex. This results in an increase in the gas velocity. This increase can be calculated by a contraction coefficient which is a function of the loading ratio and the relative width of the cyclone inlet.
