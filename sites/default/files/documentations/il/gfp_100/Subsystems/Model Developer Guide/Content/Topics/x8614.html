High-index DAEs, initialisation and integration

This section is aimed at the interested reader who would like a more detailed mathematical analysis of the causes of high index and how it may be avoided or remedied. None of the content here is required for an understanding of how gPROMS reports and rectifies high-index problems, so it is safe to ignore this section. However, a thorough understanding of index is likely to be benefitial for any modeller of chemical (or of many other) processes. Furthermore, although gPROMS can reformulate most high-index problems automatically, this can be quite computationally intensive, so it is preferrable to reduce any high-index models to index 1 by hand.
