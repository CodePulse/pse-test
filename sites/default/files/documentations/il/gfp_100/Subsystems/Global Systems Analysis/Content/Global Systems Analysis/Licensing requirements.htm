Licensing requirements

GSA and HPC are licensed features. Please contact your account manager to obtain the necessary licenses.

  • Global System Analysis (GSA) license feature, to execute GSA with a minimum of one worker
  • High-performance computing (HPC), to execute GSA and Parameter Estimation activities with up to 32 workers for parallel computation.

Note that using multiple workers does not require additional licenses of any other licensed capabilities, such as physical properties packages or Model Libraries.
