Entity comparison

When two Entities are compared, a comparison window will pop-up highlighting the differences between them (e.g. any lines that are different in the two Entities, or which have been added or deleted). The colours used for the highlighting can be configured as part of the user's gPROMS FormulatedProducts Preferences by highlighting the "Text comparison" entry in the Preferences dialog.

Comparison of two Entities

Please note that the comparison can be configured to ignore white spaces or differences in capitalisation. If enabled, only differences in actual content will be found and displayed. The same holds true for Project comparison.
