Specifications

The user-configurable specifications, which are found in the specification dialog, are listed in the table below.

Name

Default unit

Description

Status

1 or 0

Enter 1 to turn the unit on, and 0 to turn the unit off

Steam production

t/h

The mass flowrate of steam produced by the boiler

Steam pressure

bar

The steam pressure

Steam temperature

°C

The steam temperature

Efficiency specification

Selector

Choose whether to specify a fixed efficiency or enter a lookup table

Boiler efficiency

%

The boiler efficiency. In the case of a lookup table, enter the efficiencies alongside the corresponding steam production

Flue gas recycle

%

The proportion of flue gases that are recycled back to the boiler

Exhaust oxygen

%

The percentage by mass of oxygen in the flue gases

Fan power coefficients

MW, MW/t

Power requirement to operate the gas fans and blowers, comprised of a fixed component (MW) and a variable component (MW/t)

Blowdown loss

%

Percentage of steam raised that is lost via blowdown of the steam drum

The lookup table for boiler efficiency can be copied and pasted from existing data and should be a list of steam production rates (t/h) and corresponding boiler efficiencies (%). The data provided is fitted to a quadratic function of the form Efficiency = a0 + a1F + a2 F2. A minimum of 10 data points are recommended to achieve a suitable fit and the data points must cover the full valid operating range of the boiler.
