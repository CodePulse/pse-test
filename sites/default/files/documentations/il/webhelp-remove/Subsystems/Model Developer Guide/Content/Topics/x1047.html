The UNIT section

The first item of information required to set up a dynamic simulation activity is the process equipment under investigation. This is declared in the UNIT section of a PROCESS.

Equipment items are declared as instances of Models. For example

UNIT 
 T101 AS BufferTank

creates an instance of MODELBufferTank, named T101. T101 is described by the variables declared within the BufferTank Model and its time-dependent behaviour is partially determined by the corresponding equations.
