Locating declarations

The declaration of Units, Variables, Parameters, Selector names and values, Variable Types and Distribution Domains declared in a specific Model and all its sub-models, as well as in cross-referenced entities, can quickly be accessed by right-clicking on an element in an Entity editor.

In the example below, a right-click on Twall directly opens the declaration of the Variable Twall in the Reactor Model.
