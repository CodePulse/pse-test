Common options

The menus at the top of the specification dialog are used to select outlet material and calculation type specifications.

Name Default Unit Description

Outlet material

Selector
Same as inlet material
The same physical property package is used in the inlet and the outlet material streams of the model.
Different from inlet material
The physical property package for the outlet material stream is specified in this dialog and may differ from the inlet physical property package.
Outlet components Selector
Same as inlet components (including components order)
The outlet material stream components are identical to the inlet material stream components and ordered in the same manner.
Fewer than inlet components
The outlet material stream components are a subset of the inlet material stream components. The inlet components that do not belong to the subset of outlet components are assigned a component flowrate in the side outlet material stream equal to the component flowrate in the inlet material stream.
More than inlet components (no flow)
The outlet material stream components are a superset of the inlet material stream components. The outlet components that do not belong to the subset of inlet components are assigned a zero component flowrate in the outlet material stream.
More than inlet components (from port)
The outlet material stream components are a superset of the inlet material stream components. The outlet components that do not belong to the subset of inlet components are assigned a component flowrate in the outlet material stream equal to the component flowrate in the side inlet material stream.
Calculation type Selector
Isothermal
Sets the temperature in the outlet material stream and in the side outlet material stream equal to the temperature of the inlet material stream.
Adiabatic
The temperature of the outlet and side outlet material streams is equal to the temperature determined by an energy balance that assumes adiabatic mixing.
