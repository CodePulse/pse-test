Specifications

The model specification dialog of this model comprises the following tabs:

Name

Description
Design (geometry) Basic geometrical design of the bed. Define bed and layer sizing here.

Design (adsorbent)

 Loading of the adsorbent and geometry of the adsorbent pellets.
Fluid (mass transfer) Parameters related to mass transfer in the fluid phase and from the fluid phase to the adsorbed phase.

Fluid (heat transfer)

 Parameters related to heat transfer in the fluid phase and from the fluid phase to the adsorbed phase and to the bed wall.
Design (wall) Thickness and material of the bed wall.
Isotherm Definition of the adsorption isotherm.
Dynamics Initial conditions in the bed.
Numerics Parameters related to the solution algorithm for the bed model.
