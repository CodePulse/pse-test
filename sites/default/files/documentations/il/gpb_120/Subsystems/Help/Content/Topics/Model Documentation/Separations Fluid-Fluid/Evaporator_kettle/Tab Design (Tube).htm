Design (Tube)

The Design (Tube) tab is used to specify tube shape/dimension data.

Name Default Unit Description
Tube design Selector
Off
In this mode no tube geometry specifications are required and therefore the tube fluid is operating under steady-state conditions.
On
In this mode tube geometry specifications are required and therefore mass and energy holdups may be included in the hot stream balance equations.
Channel shape Selector
Cylinder
Selects cylinder shape for the tube.
Abstract shape
Selects an abstract shape for the tube. Volume and area of the abstract shape must be provided.

Channel radius specification

 Selector
Inner diameter
Determines the inner tube radius from its diameter.
Radius
Specifies the inner tube radius directly.
Cross-sectional area
Determines the inner tube radius from its cross-sectional area.
Determine from volume specification
Determines the inner tube radius by specifying its volume.

Channel length specification

 Selector
Length
Specifies the tube length directly.
Determine from volume specification
Determines the tube length from the volume specification.
Number of channels -

Assigns the number of channels. The channel model can be used to represent a single channel or a multichannel configuration. In case of a multi-channel configuration it is assumed that the flow is distributed uniformly over the channels.

Diameter

 m Inner tube diameter.

Radius

 m Tube inner radius.

Cross sectional area

 m2 Tube inner cross sectional area.

Length

 m Tube length.

Volume

 m3 Inner tube volume.
Surface area m2

Value of the surface area of the abstract shape. This is the area used for heat transfer through the channel wall.
Equivalent diameter m Value of the equivalent diameter of the abstract shape.
