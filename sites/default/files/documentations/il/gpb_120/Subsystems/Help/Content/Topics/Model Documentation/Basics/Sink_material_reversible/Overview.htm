Sink material reversible

The Sink material reversible model is the point where a material stream leaves the flowsheet. The pressure has to be specified at this point.

This model is able to handle flow-reversal. Flow-reversal occurs when the pressure at the Sink material reversible is higher than the pressure upstream of this model. When the flow reverses the Sink material model reversible will acts as a Source material. Stream conditions (temperature and composition) need to be specified in the Sink material reversible model. These stream conditions will be used to determine the conditions of the stream flowing out of the sink when flow-reversal occurs.
