Design

The Design tab is used to insert design data.

Name Default Unit Description
Geometry specification Selector
Off
The geometry of the unit is not set. For steady-state simulations specification of the geometry is generally not required.
On
Specifies the geometry of the unit. For dynamic simulations this may be a more appropriate choice as it will permit the calculation of material holdups in this unit.

Shape

 Selector
Abstract shape
Selects an abstract shape for the unit. The volume and area of the abstract shape can be provided.
Drum
Selects a drum shape for the unit.

Specification of radius of cylindrical part of the sump vessel

 Selector
Diameter
Determines the vessel radius from its diameter.
Radius
Specifies the vessel radius directly.
Cross-sectional area
Determines the vessel radius from its cross-sectional area.
Determine from volume or area specification
Determines the vessel radius by specifying its volume or surface area.

Specification of length (tan-to-tan) of the sump vessel

 Selector
Length
Specifies the length of the vessel, directly.
Determine from volume or area specification
Determines the length of the vessel from the volume or surface area specification.

Vessel heads shape

 Selector
Flat
Selects flat vessel heads.
Hemispherical
Selects vessel heads with hemispherical shape.
Ellipsoidal
Selects vessel heads with ellipsoidal shape.
Torispherical
Selects vessel heads with torispherical shape.
Specification of radius of elliptical part of vessel Selector
Radius ellipsoid
Specifies the radius of the elliptical part of the vessel.
Determine from volume or area specification
Determines the radius of the elliptical part of the vessel from the volume or surface area specification.

Drum overall specification

 Selector
Volume
Uses the total volume of the vessel for the geometry calculation.
Area
Uses the surface area of the vessel for the geometry calculation.

 m
