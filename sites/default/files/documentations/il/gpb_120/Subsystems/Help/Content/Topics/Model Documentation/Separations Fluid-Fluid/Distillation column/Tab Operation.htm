Operation

The Operation tab is used to specify the operating conditions of the column.

Name Default Unit Description
Reboiler temperature Selector
No superheating
The vapour leaving the reboiler is at the equilibrium temperature.
Superheating (specify Degree of superheating)
Vapour leaving the reboiler is superheated. The value of the temperature difference is set in the dialog to specify the degree of superheating.
Superheating (specify reboiler temperature)
Vapour leaving the reboiler is superheated. This option uses the reboiler temperature specified in the dialog.

Condenser temperature

Selector
No subcooling
Liquid leaving the condenser is at the equilibrium temperature.
Subcooling (specify Degree of subcooling)
Liquid leaving the condenser is subcooled. The value of the temperature difference is set in the dialog to specify the degree of subcooling.
Subcooling (specify condenser temperature)
Liquid leaving the condenser is subcooled. This option uses the condenser temperature specified in the dialog.

Specification 1, 2 and 3 basis Selector
Molar
Specifications are on a molar basis.
Mass
Specifications are on a mass basis.
Boilup ratio mol/mol or kg/kg Ratio of flowrate of liquid that is re-boiled as vapour (V) to the amount of liquid leaving the reboiler (B).
Boilup ratio normalized mol/mol or kg/kg Ratio of flowrate of liquid that is re-boiled as vapour (V) to the total amount leaving the reboiler (V+B).
Reflux ratio mol/mol or kg/kg Ratio of flowrate of liquid that is refluxed back (R) to liquid distillate flowrate (D) plus vapour distillate flowrate (VT).
Reflux ratio normalized mol/mol or kg/kg Ratio of flowrate of liquid that is refluxed back (R) to the total vapour flow entering the condenser.
Top vapour product flowrate kmol/h or kg/s Flowrate of the vapour leaving the condenser as distillate.
Top liquid product flowrate kmol/h or kg/s Flowrate of the liquid leaving the condenser as distillate.
Bottom product flowrate kmol/h or kg/s Flowrate of the liquid product at the bottom of the column.
Top vapour product flowrate to feed flowrate ratio mol/mol or kg/kg Ratio of the flowrates of the vapour distillate to the feed.
Top liquid product flowrate to feed flowrate ratio mol/mol or kg/kg Ratio of the flowrates of the liquid distillate to the feed.
Bottom product flowrate to feed flowrate ratio mol/mol or kg/kg Ratio of the flowrates of the bottom to the feed.
Component for top vapour purity specification - Component for which the purity is specified.
Top vapour product purity mol/mol or kg/kg Purity of the top vapour product.
Component for top liquid purity specification - Component for which the purity is specified.
Top liquid product purity mol/mol or kg/kg Purity of the top liquid product.
Component for bottom purity specification - Component for which the purity is specified.
Bottom product purity mol/mol or kg/kg Purity of the bottom product.
Component for top vapour recovery specification - Component for which the recovery is specified.
Top vapour product recovery - Ratio of the flowrate of the specified component into the vapour product to that in the feed.
Component for top liquid recovery specification - Selects the component for which the recovery is specified.
Top liquid product recovery - Ratio of the flowrate of the specified component into the top liquid product to that in the feed.
Component for bottom recovery specification - Selects the component for which the recovery is specified.
Bottom product recovery - Ratio of the flowrate of the specified component into the bottom product to that in the feed.
Reboiler duty kJ/s Heat duty of the reboiler (this should be positive).
Reboiler temperature K Reboiler temperature.
Condenser duty kJ/s Heat duty of the condenser (this should be negative).
Condenser temperature K Condenser temperature.
Reflux valve flow coefficient kg s-1 Pa-1 Flow coefficient of the reflux valve located on the liquid line going from the condenser to the top tray, just after the split for the top liquid product.
Reflux molar flowrate kmol/h Molar flowrate of the liquid coming from the condenser and being fed back to the column on the top tray.
Reflux mass flowrate kg/s Mass flowrate of the liquid coming from the condenser and being fed back to the column on the top tray.
