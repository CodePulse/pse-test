D05 - VAM reactor

Vinyl acetate is the most important vinyl ester and is used in the production of polymers and co-polymers. It can be used to produce end-products such as paints, coatings, adhesives, textile and paper processing, and others.

Vynil acetate monomer main production route is the gas-phase reaction of ethylene with acetic acid and oxygen in fixed-bed reactors. Since the reaction is exothermic, the fixed-bed reactors employed are cooled with boiling water, thus producing steam. This example illustrates the application of the AML:FBCR with a boiling water cooling section to the vinyl acetate production process.
