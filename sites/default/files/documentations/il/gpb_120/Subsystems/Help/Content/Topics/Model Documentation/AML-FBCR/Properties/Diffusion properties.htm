Diffusion properties

Gas-phase binary diffusivities are calculated according to Fuller, Schettler and Giddings (Ind. Eng. Chem., 58, 19, 1966). The molecular diffusion volumes can be approximated by summing up the volumes of the individual atoms in the molecule , which are included in the tables below.

Atomic Diffusion Volumes (from Table 11.1 of Poling, Prausnitz, O'Connell: The Properties of Gases and Liquids, 5th ed., McGraw-Hill 2004).
