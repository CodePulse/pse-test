Design (Shell wall)

The Design (Shell wall) tab is used to specify shell wall shape/dimension data.

Name Default Unit Description
Heat losses through shell wall Selector
Off
If no heat transfer to the surroundings occurs.
On
The contribution of the heat transfer to the surroundings is included in the energy balances.
Shell wall design Selector
Off
In this mode no shell wall geometry specifications are required and therefore the shell wall is operating under steady-state conditions.
On
In this mode shell wall geometry specifications are required and therefore mass and energy holdups may be included in balance equations.
Shape Selector
Drum
Selects a drum shape for the unit.
