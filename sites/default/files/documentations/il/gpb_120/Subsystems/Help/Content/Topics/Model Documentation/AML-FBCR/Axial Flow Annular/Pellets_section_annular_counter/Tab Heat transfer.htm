Heat transfer

The heat transfer mode and coefficients, the wall geometry and the wall properties are specified in the Heat transfer tab.

Name Default Unit Description
Radial heat transfer medium Selector
Solid
Heat from the wall is exchanged directly with the solid phase. Radial conduction occurs only in the solid phase.
Fluid
Heat from the wall is exchanged directly with the fluid phase. Radial conduction occurs only in the fluid phase.
Fluid and solid
Heat from the wall is exchanged directly with both the fluid and the solid phases. Radial conduction occurs in both phases.
Axial heat conduction Selector
Off
Switches off the axial conduction in the catalyst bed.
On
Takes into account the axial conduction in the catalyst bed.
