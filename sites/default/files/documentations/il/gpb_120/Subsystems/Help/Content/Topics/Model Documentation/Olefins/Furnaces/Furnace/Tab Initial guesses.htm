Initial guesses

The Initial guesses tab is used to specify data pertaining to model initialisation.

Name Default Unit Description
Heat flux Selector
Specified
Allows the specification of initial guess for radiant coil heat flux.
Normalised to feed mass flowrate
Allows the specification of initial guess of average radiant coil heat flux normalised to hydrocarbon feed mass flowrate per coil.
Radiant coil passes - outlet temperatures °C

Initial guess for outlet temperature of each coil pass.
TLE outlet temperature °C

Initial guess for TLE outlet temperature .
Radiant coil heat flux kW/m2 Initial guess for average heat flux to the radiant coil.
Radiant coil heat flux per feed mass flowrate (kW/m2) / (kg/s) / coil Initial guess for average radiant coil heat flux normalised to the hydrocarbon feed mass flowrate per coil.
