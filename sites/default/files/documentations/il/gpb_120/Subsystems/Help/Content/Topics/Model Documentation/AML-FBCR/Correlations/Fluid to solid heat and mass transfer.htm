Fluid-to-solid heat and mass transfer

The AML:FBCR catalyst bed models consider fluid-to-particle interfacial heat and mass transfer resistance. The heat transfer resistance in the bed is described using the fluid-solid heat transfer coefficient and the mass transfer resistance using the fluid-solid mass transfer coefficient .
