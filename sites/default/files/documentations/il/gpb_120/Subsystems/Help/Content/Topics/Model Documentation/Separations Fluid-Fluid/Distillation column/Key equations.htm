Mass and energy balance

For each tray the model includes a mass and energy balance. The vapour and the liquid on the tray are assumed to be in equilibrium. The tray mass and energy balances, together with the equations that describe the equilibrium, are referred to as the MESH equations: material balances (M), phase equilibrium relations (E), mole fraction summations (S) and an energy balance (H).
