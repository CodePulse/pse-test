Key equations

The Activated Sludge Models (ASMs) used to compute the biochemical reactions are described in the section 'Activated Sludge Models (ASMs)' The documentation for the model used to compute the pH and the electrolytes system can be found at 'Electrolyte system equilibriums'

The components involved in the mass balances are specific to the activated sludge and water chemistry model in use. This model assume a constant water volume and a constant pressure and consider the gas-liquid mass transfer from the liquid to the atmosphere only (no mass transfer in the other direction is currently possible).

Mass reaction rate

The mass reaction rate for each component j is the difference between the net component production rate by biochemical reactions in the ASM sub-model and the liquid-gas transfer rate.
