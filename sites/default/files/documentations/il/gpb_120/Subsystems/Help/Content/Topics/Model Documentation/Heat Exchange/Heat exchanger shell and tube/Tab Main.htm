The Main tab is used to specify data relating to the geometry and operation of the heat exchanger.

Flow arrangement Selector

The flow of the tube side fluid and the shell side fluid is co-current in the axial direction.
Counter-current
The flow of the tube side fluid and the shell side fluid is counter-current in the axial direction.

Type E of Tubular Exchanger Manufacturers Association. In this configuration one shell pass and up to 16 tube side passes are allowed.
Tubes in baffle window Selector

Selects the use of tubes in baffle windows.

Selects a geometry without tubes in baffle windows.

Tube bundle type Selector
Straight
The area for heat transfer and length for pressure drop calculations do not take into account the U-bend.
U-tubes
Both the surface area and the length of the U-bend are taken into account for heat transfer and pressure drop calculations.

Number of tubes - The number of tubes.

Diameter of outer tube limit (OTL) m The diameter created by encircling the outer most tubes in a tube layout.
