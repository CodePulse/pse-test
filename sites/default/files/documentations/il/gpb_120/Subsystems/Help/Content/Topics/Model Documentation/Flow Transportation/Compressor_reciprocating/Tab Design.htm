Design

The Design tab is used to specify compressor sizing data, enabling design speed and rod load calculations. This tab can be turned off.

Name Default Unit Description
Piston type Selector
Single-acting (outer compression)
Single-acting cylinder with head-end displacement.
Single-acting (crank compression)
Single-acting cylinder with crank-end displacement.
Double-acting (other than tail rod type)
Double-acting cylinder where the piston rod is provided without a tail rod.
Double-acting (tail rod type)
Double-acting cylinder where the piston rod is provided with a tail rod.
Volumetric efficiency Selector
Ideal compressor
The volumetric efficiency is calculated as a function of the cylinder clearance, the compression ratio and the properties of the gas being compressed.
Real compressor - specified
The volumetric efficiency is specified by the user.
Number of cylinders - Number of cylinders in the compressor.
Clearance volume (outer side) m3 Head-end clearance volume remaining in the compressor cylinder at the end of the stroke.
Clearance volume (crank side) m3 Crank-end clearance volume remaining in the compressor cylinder at the end of the stroke.
Piston diameter m Diameter of the pistons
Piston rod diameter m Diameter of the piston rod
Stroke length m Sets the stroke length.
Constant volumetric efficiency loss % Constant volumetric efficiency losses that decrease the volumetric efficiency.
Volumetric efficiency % Volumetric efficiency (i.e. the ratio of the actual volume of gas drawn into the cylinder to the piston displacement volume).
