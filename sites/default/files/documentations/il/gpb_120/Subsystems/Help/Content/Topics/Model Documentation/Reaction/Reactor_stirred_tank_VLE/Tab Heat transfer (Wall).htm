Heat transfer (Wall)

The Heat transfer (Wall) tab is used to calculate the heat transferred between the unit and the surrounding environment.

Name Default Unit Description
Wall to surroundings heat transfer coefficient Selector
Specify
Specifies the shell wall to surroundings heat transfer coefficient directly.
Custom
Use Custom Modelling option to provide a heat transfer coefficient.
Reporting heat losses Selector
Off
Turns off the option to report heat losses to the surroundings.
On
Turns on the option to report heat losses to the surroundings.
Heat transfer correlation Selector
Custom
User-supplied equations in a custom model are given to determine the heat transfer coefficient.
Surroundings temperature K Temperature of the surrounding environment.
Wall to surroundings heat transfer coefficient W m-2 K-1
Vessel wall to surrounding environment heat transfer coefficient.
