Specifications

The Double-pipe heat exchanger model specification dialog comprises the following tabs:

Name

Description
Main Specifications related to the geometry and operation of the heat exchanger.
Dynamics Specifications related to dynamic simulation.

Design (Inner pipe)

Specifications related to the inner pipe design.
Pressure (Inner pipe) Specifications related to the inner pipe pressure.

Heat transfer (Inner pipe)

 Specifications related to the inner pipe heat transfer.
Dynamics (Inner pipe) Specifications related to the inner pipe dynamic initial conditions.

Design (Outer pipe)

Specifications related to the outer pipe design.
Pressure (Outer pipe) Specifications related to the outer pipe pressure.

Heat transfer (Outer pipe)

 Specifications related to the outer pipe heat transfer.
Dynamics (Outer pipe) Specifications related to the outer pipe dynamic initial conditions.

Heat transfer (Surroundings)

 Specifications related to heat losses to the surroundings.

Numerics

 Specifications related to numerical parameters.
