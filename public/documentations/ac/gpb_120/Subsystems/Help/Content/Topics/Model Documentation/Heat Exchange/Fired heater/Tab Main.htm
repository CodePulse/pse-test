Saturated liquid (Vapour fraction = 0)
When selecting this option, the outlet stream will consist of a saturated liquid (i.e. the outlet stream temperature is the bubble temperature of the mixture).
Saturated vapour (Vapour fraction = 1)
When selecting this option, the outlet stream will consist of a saturated vapour (i.e. the outlet stream temperature is the dew temperature of the mixture).
Material (Combustion)  

Material definition for the combustion mixture.
Thermal efficiency % Thermal efficiency. This is the amount of heat added to the process fluid divided by the amount of heat generated by the fuel combustion.

Subcooling ΔT K

The difference between the saturated liquid temperature (bubble temperature) and the actual temperature of the outlet stream .
Superheating ΔT K

The difference between the actual temperature of the outlet stream and the saturated vapour temperature (dew temperature).
