Tray holdup correlations

To define the accumulation terms in the material and energy dynamic equations the liquid and vapour holdups must be considered. The effective clear liquid height hc: is the height to which the aerated mass on the trays would collapse in the absence of vapour flow. This height gives a measure of the liquid level on the tray and can be used to calculate the liquid molar holdup for a given stage. The equations presented in the remainder of this section apply to each stage. The subscript j has been omitted for the purpose of clarity.
