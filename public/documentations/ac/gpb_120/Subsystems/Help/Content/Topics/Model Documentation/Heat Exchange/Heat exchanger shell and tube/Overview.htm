Heat exchanger shell and tube

This unit models a shell and tube heat exchanger in which heat is exchanged between two material streams. This detailed distributed model can be used to simulate the behaviour of a real shell and tube heat exchanger (at the moment limited to one shell pass) in both steady-state and dynamic modes, by providing input stream conditions, as well as detailed geometrical data for the unit. The heat transfer coefficients can be specified for each fluid, or calculated using a built-in or custom correlation. Both the effects of fouling and heat losses to the surroundings can be taken into account.
