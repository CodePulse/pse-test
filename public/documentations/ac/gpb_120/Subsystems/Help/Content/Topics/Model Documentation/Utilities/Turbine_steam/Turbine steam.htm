Turbine steam

The Turbine steam model can be used to represent power generation via steam expansion. The Turbine steam has inlet and outlet streams for steam, and an outlet power stream. The model is configured by specifying the status, mass flowrate, efficiency and outlet pressure. The model icon, as it appears on the Palette, is shown below.
