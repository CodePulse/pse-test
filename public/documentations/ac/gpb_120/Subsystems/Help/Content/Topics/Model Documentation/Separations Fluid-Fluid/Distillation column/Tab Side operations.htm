Side operations

The Side operations tab is used to specify data relative to the side draws of the column.

Name Default Unit Description
Liquid side draw specification basis Selector
Molar
Specifies the liquid side draw(s) by their molar flowrate(s).
Mass
Specifies the liquid side draw(s) by their mass flowrate(s).
Ratio (U / L)
Specifies the ratio of the liquid side draw flowrate to the flowrate of the liquid going to the stage below (this can be higher than 1).
Vapour side draw specification basis Selector
Molar
Specifies the vapour side draw(s) by their molar flowrate(s).
Mass
Specifies the vapour side draw(s) by their mass flowrate(s).
Ratio (W / V)
Specifies the ratio of the vapour side draw flowrate to the flowrate of the vapour going to the stage above (this can be higher than 1).
Liquid side draw position - Position of the liquid side-draw(s).

 -
Position of the vapour side-draw(s).
Liquid side draw molar flowrate kmol/h Molar flowrate(s) of the liquid side draw(s).
Vapour side draw molar flowrate kmol/h Molar flowrate(s) of the vapour side draw(s).
Liquid side draw mass flowrate kmol/h Mass flowrate(s) of the liquid side draw(s).
Vapour side draw mass flowrate kmol/h Mass flowrate(s) of the vapour side draw(s).
Liquid side draw ratio (U / L) -

Ratio of the liquid side draw flowrate to the flowrate of the liquid going to the stage below (this can be higher than 1).
Vapour side draw ratio (W / V) -

Ratio of the vapour side draw flowrate to the flowrate of the vapour going to the stage above (this can be higher than 1).
