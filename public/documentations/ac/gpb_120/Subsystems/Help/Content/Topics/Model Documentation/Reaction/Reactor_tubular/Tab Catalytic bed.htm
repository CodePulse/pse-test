Catalytic bed

The Catalytic bed tab is used to specify the bed and pellets properties.

Catalyst mass per tube kg Mass of catalyst present in one tube.
Pellet bulk density kg/m3 Bulk density of the pellets.

Pellet radius m Radius of the pellets.
Pellet length m Length of the pellets.
Pellet hollow radius m Inner radius of the hollow in hollow shaped pellets.
Number of hollows - Number of hollows for multi-hollow cylinder pellets.
