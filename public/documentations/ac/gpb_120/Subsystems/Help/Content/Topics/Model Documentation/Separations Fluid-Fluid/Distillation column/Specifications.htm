Specifications

The Distillation column model specification dialog has the following tabs:

Name

Specifications for
Column the number of stages, the position of the feed(s) and of the eventual side-draws.

Design (Column geometry)

the column height and diameter.

Design (Condenser)

 the geometry of the condenser.

Design (Reboiler)

the geometry of the reboiler.

Design (Internals)

the geometry of the trays.
Pressure the pressure profile.

Operation

the operating conditions.

Dynamics

dynamic simulation.

Side operations the side draw(s).
Heaters/coolers the stage heater and cooler.
Tray efficiencies the efficiency of the trays.
Costing

the equipment cost calculations.

Initial guesses

the initial guesses used to initialise the unit model and the flowsheet.

Connectivity the external specification for the feed(s) and side draw(s).
Reporting

the reporting options.

Numerics

the numerical parameters.
