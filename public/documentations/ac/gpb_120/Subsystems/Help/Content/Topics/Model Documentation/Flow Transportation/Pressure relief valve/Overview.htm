Pressure relief valve

The Pressure relief valve model describes the behaviour of a pressure relief valve/pressure safety valve. The valve can be sized either for a discharge coefficient or a discharge area, using the available correlations for vapour or liquid and the desired characteristic curve. These determine the flowrate when the valve opens, which is not expected to occur during normal operation.

By selecting the correct design options, this valve model can be used to represent various types of commonly used pressure relief valves/pressure safety valves such as ball conventional spring-operated, balanced-bellows or pilot-operated valves.
