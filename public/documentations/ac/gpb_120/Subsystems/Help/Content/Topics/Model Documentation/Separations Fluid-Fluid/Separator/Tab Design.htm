DIN torispherical (L=OD; r=0.1xOD)

Selects a torispherical head with a Crown radius equal to outer diameter of the vessel and a knuckle radius equal to 10% of the outer diameter. This head type is also know as a Kl√∂pper head.

DIN semi ellipsoidal (L=0.8xOD; r=0.154xOD)

Selects a torispherical head with a Crown radius equal to 80% of outer diameter of the vessel and a knuckle radius equal to 15.4% of the outer diameter. This head type is also know as a Korbbogen head.

ASME torispherical (L=OD; r_min=0.06xOD)

Selects a torispherical head with a Crown radius equal to outer diameter of the vessel. The knuckle radius can be specified via the knuckle radius factor (minimum of 6% of the vessel outer diameter).
