Operation

The Operation tab is used with the Design (Shell) tab to insert the cold stream data.

Name Default Unit Description
Thermal specification Selector
Heat duty
Selects specification of a transferred heat duty to the cold stream.
Temperature
Selects specification of the cold stream outlet temperature.
Vapour fraction
Selects specification of the cold stream outlet vapour fraction (vapour stream flowrate divided by the sum of the outlet flowrates).
Temperature difference
Selects specification of the temperature difference between the outlet cold streams and the inlet cold stream.
Enthalpy
Selects specification of the cold stream global outlet mass specific enthalpy.
Duty specification Selector
Specified here
Selects specification of a transferred heat duty (to the cold stream).
From energy connection
The heat duty supplied to the cold stream is obtained externally using a model connected to the gMLEnergy port.
Liquid level specification Selector
Equivalent to feed phase fraction
The liquid level fraction inside the shell is equal to the cold stream inlet feed phase vapour fraction.
Specified here
The liquid level in the shell is specified internally (Operation tab).
Liquid level fraction specification basis Selector
Liquid level fraction
Selects liquid level fraction specification.
Liquid volume fraction
Selects liquid volume fraction specification.
Liquid phase fraction on mass basis
Selects liquid phase fraction on mass basis specification.
Liquid level fraction m m-1 Assigns the fraction of liquid level in the shell to the shell height.
Liquid volume fraction m3 m-3 Assigns the fraction of liquid volume in the shell to the shell volume.
Liquid phase fraction on mass basis kg/kg Assigns the fraction of liquid mass in the shell to the total mass inside the shell.
