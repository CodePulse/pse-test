Costing

The Costing tab is used to provide equipment cost calculation data.

Name Default Unit Description
Cost calculations Selector
Off
Turns off equipment cost calculation.
On
Turns on equipment cost calculation.
Material of construction Selector
Cast iron
Selects cast iron material of construction.
Bronze
Selects bronze material of construction.
Cast steel
Selects cast steel material of construction.
Ductile
Selects ductile iron material of construction.
Hastelloy C
Selects hastelloy C material of construction.
Monel
Selects monel material of construction.
Nickel
Selects nickel material of construction.
SS316L
Selects stainless steel material of construction.
Titanium
Selects titanium material of construction.
Motor type Selector
Open drip proof enclosure
Selects an open drip proof enclosure motor enclosure designed to prevent the entrance of liquid and dirt particles into the working parts of the motor.
Explosion proof
Selects an explosion proof motor enclosure which protects the motor against explosion hazards.
TEFC
Selects a totally enclosed fan-cooled motor enclosure which prevents air from getting inside.
CE index - Chemical engineering plant cost index (CEPCI).
Pump type factor - Assigns the pump type factor which varies with the pump shaft, flow rate, head range, maximum motor and case-split orientation.
