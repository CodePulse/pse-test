Design (Internals)

The Design (Internals) tab is used to specify data relative to the internal elements geometry. This tab can be turned off and on from the Design selector in the Design (Column geometry) tab.

Active area fraction

 -

Tower cross-section area, less total downcomer area, seal downcomer area and any other non-perforated regions (often referred as bubbling area) to total tower cross-section area ratio. Represents the area available to vapour flow near the tray floor.

Hole area fraction

 -

Ratio of the hole area to bubbling area. Note that the hole area is the total area of perforations on the tray.

Weir fraction

 -

Weir length to column diameter ratio.
