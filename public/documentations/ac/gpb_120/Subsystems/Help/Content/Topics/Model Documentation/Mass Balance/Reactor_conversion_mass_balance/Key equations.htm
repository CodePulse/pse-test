Key equations

Mass balance

In the conversion reactor the reactions can be selected to occur in parallel or in series. In case of parallel reactions the conversion of each reaction is applied to the reactor inlet composition. In case of serial conversions the conversion of the first reaction is applied to the reactor inlet composition and the conversions of subsequent reactions are applied to the composition after the effects of the preceding reaction has been taken into account.
