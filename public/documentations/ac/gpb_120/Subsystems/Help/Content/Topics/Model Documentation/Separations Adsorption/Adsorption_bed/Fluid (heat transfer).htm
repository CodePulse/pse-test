Fluid (heat transfer)

The Fluid (heat transfer) tab is used to specify the heat transfer properties for the fluid phase.

Name Default Unit Description
Thermal operation mode Selector
Non-isothermal operation
Predict changes in temperature in the bed from the energy balance.
Isothermal operation
Assume isothermal operation, with the bed at a constant specified temperature.

Bed effective heat conductivity

Selector
Calculated (Specchia,1980)
Predict the effective heat conductivity for the fluid phase from the Specchia correlation.
Custom
Custom effective heat conductivity correlation. Use the custom model in gML Templates to code the custom correlation using the gPROMS ProcessBuilder Custom Modelling feature.
No heat conduction
Assume there is no heat conduction in the flow direction both in the fluid and in the adsorbent material.

Heat transfer coefficient between bed-wall

 Selector
No heat loss to wall
Assume there are no heat losses through the wall.
Fixed
Fixed heat transfer coefficient between the bed and the wall.

Heat transfer coefficient between bed - wall

 W m-2K-1

Value for the heat transfer coefficient between the bed and the bed wall.
Heat transfer between fluid and adsorbent Selector
Fluid and adsorbent at the same temperature
The fluid and the adsorbent material are at the same temperature. It is assumed that heat transfer is infinitely fast.
Predict heat transfer between fluid and adsorbent.
Prediction of heat transfer between fluid and adsorbent.
Heat transfer coefficient between fluid and adsorbent Selector
Fixed
The heat transfer coefficient has a fixed value.
Gnielinski (1980)
The Gnielinski correlation is used to predict heat transfer between the fluid and the solid phase.
Hougen
The Hougen correlation is used to predict heat transfer between the fluid and the solid phase.
Custom
Custom correlation for the heat transfer coefficient between fluid phase and the solid. Use the custom model in gML Templates to code the custom correlation using the gPROMS ProcessBuilder Custom Modelling feature.
Heat transfer coefficient between fluid and adsorbent W m-2K-1 Value for the fixed heat transfer coefficient between the fluid phase and the adsorbent.
