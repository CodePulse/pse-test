Secondary Point Settler

This simple model represents a secondary separation process. It separates the settleable (particulate) matter, i.e. the "X" state variables, from the non-settleable (soluble) matter, i.e. the "S" compounds. Two separation efficiencies are specified (biomass and suspended solids), as well as the sludge extraction flowrate (WAS), and the sludge recycle flowrate (RAS).
