Design

The Design tab is used to specify data relative to the shape and dimensions of the tube(s).

Name Default Unit Description
Thermal operation Selector
Adiabatic
Adiabatic operation. The reactor is operated adiabatically, the shell side (in case of reactions in the tube) is not modelled.
Fixed heat duty. The shell side (in case of reactions in the tube) is not modelled.
Fixed heat duty supplied to the reactor. The shell side (in case of reactions in the tube) is not modelled.
Heat exchanged with surroundings
Heat exchange with surroundings. Surroundings are at a fixed ambient temperature. The shell side (in case of reactions in the tube) is not modelled.
Heat exchanged with utility at fixed temperature
Heat exchange with utility in shell side, which is assumed to be at fixed temperature. The shell side (in case of reactions in the tube) is not modelled.
Heat exchanged with utility
Heat exchange with utility in shell side. The shell side (in case of reactions in the tube) mass and energy balances are included in the model. Requires a utility stream to be connected to the shell inlet port of the model.
Utility placement Selector
Shellside
The reactions occur in the tube. The utility fluid is on the shell-side.
Tubeside
The reactions occur in the shell. The utility fluid is on the tube-side.
Flow arrangement Selector

Co-current

The flow of the reaction fluid and the utility fluid is co-current in the axial direction.
Counter-current
The flow of the reaction fluid and the utility fluid is counter-current in the axial direction.
Channel shape Selector
Cylinder
Selects cylinder shape for the channel.
Abstract shape
Selects an abstract shape for the unit. Volume and area of the abstract shape must be provided.
Wall shape Selector
Hollow cylinder
Selects cylinder shape for the wall.
Hollow abstract shape
Selects an abstract shape for the wall. Volume and area of the abstract shape must be provided.

 Selector

Determine from volume specification

 Selector

Outer radius
Specifies the outer radius directly.
Outer area
Determines the outer radius by specifying the outer area of the wall.
