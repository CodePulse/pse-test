Initial guesses

The Initial guesses tab is used to specify the initial specifications used during the initialisation of the column model.

Name Default Unit Description
Initial guess for pressure specification Selector
Pressure drop per stage
This selector is available when using advance hydraulics correlations. Applies a fixed pressure drop per stage while going through the column initialisation procedure. The hydraulics correlation are applied only at the last step.
Top
This selector is available when using advance hydraulics correlations. Provides one additional pressure specification to apply a linear pressure profile while going through the column initialisation procedure. The hydraulics correlation are applied only at the last step.
Bottom
This selector is available when using advance hydraulics correlations. Provides one additional pressure specification to apply a linear pressure profile while going through the column initialisation procedure. The hydraulics correlation are applied only at the last step.
Initial guess for pressure profile Selector
Linear
Uses a linear pressure profile for the initial feed flash.
Average
Uses a constant pressure profile for the initial feed flash based on the average pressure specifications given by th euser.
Initial guess for temperature profile Selector
Bubble and dew temperature
Initially assigns the bubble temperature at the top of the column and the dew temperature at the bottom of the column.
Specified
Uses the values specified in the dialog for the top and bottom temperatures.
Initial guess for composition profile Selector
Linear (total feed flash at initial T, P profile)
Flashes the combined feed at the initial stage temperature and pressure and initially assigns the obtained phase compositions to each stage in the column.
Linear (Feed flash at average column T, P and linear composition profile)
Flashes the combined feed at the initial stage temperature and pressure and initially assigns the vapour composition on the top stage, the liquid composition on the bottom stage, and establish a linear composition gradient in between. The initial guesses for the liquid and vapour phase composition on each stage are equal.
Constant (total feed flash at average column T, P)
Flashes the combined feed at the average temperature and pressure and initially assigns the obtained phase composition to each stage in the column.
Custom
Uses custom input for the liquid and vapour composition on each stage.
Include subcooled and superheated feed in CMO Selector
No
Uses a regular Constant Molar Overflow (CMO) calculation to generate flowrate initial guesses.
Yes
Takes into account the heat carried by superheated or subcooled feeds in the regular Constant Molar Overflow (CMO) calculation used to generate flowrate initial guesses.
Specification 1, 2 and 3 used for initialisation Selector
Boilup ratio ( V/B )
Boilup ratio normalized ( V/(V+B) )
Reflux ratio ( R/(D+VT) )
Reflux ratio normalized ( R/(R+D+VT) )
Top vapour product flowrate ( VT )
Top liquid product flowrate ( D )
Bottom product flowrate ( B )
Top vapour product to feed flowrate ratio ( VT/F )
Top liquid product to feed flowrate ratio ( D/F )
Bottom product to feed flowrate ratio ( B/F )
Initial guess 1, 2 and 3 specification basis Selector
Molar
Specifications are on a molar basis.
Mass
Specifications are on a mass basis.

Pressure

 Selector
Specified here (Pressure uniform over column)
Uses the pressure specified in the dialog.
From feed
Uses the pressure of the selected feed and applies it uniformly over the column.
Top and bottom pressures specified here (Linear profile, specify top and bottom pressure)
The two different pressures used for the linear profile are located at the top and the bottom of the column and are specified here.
Top pressure from feed (at location) and bottom pressure specified here
The two different pressures used for the linear profile are located at the same location as one of the feed for the top one and the bottom of the column for the bottom one. The bottom pressure is specified here.
Top pressure specified here and bottom pressure from feed (at location)
The two different pressures used for the linear profile are located at the top of the column for the top one and at the same location as one of the feed for the bottom one. The top pressure is specified here.
Specified pressure Selector
Top
The pressure is specified at the top of the column.
Bottom
The pressure is specified at the bottom of the column.
From feed
The specified pressure of the column is obtained from a selected feed.
Condenser pressure specification Selector
Liquid height
Imposes a liquid height in the condenser.
Distillate pressure
Specifies the value of the distillate pressure.
Pressure drop
Uses the pressure drop specified in the dialog.
Reboiler pressure specification Selector
Liquid height
Imposes a liquid height in the reboiler.
Bottom product pressure
Uses the bottom product pressure specified in the dialog.
Pressure drop
Uses the pressure drop specified in the dialog.
Boilup ratio mol/mol or kg/kg Ratio of flowrate of liquid that is re-boiled as vapour (V) to the amount of liquid leaving the reboiler (B).
Boilup ratio normalized mol/mol or kg/kg Ratio of flowrate of liquid that is re-boiled as vapour (V) to the total amount leaving the reboiler (V+B).
Reflux ratio mol/mol or kg/kg Ratio of flowrate of liquid that is refluxed back (R) to liquid distillate flowrate (D) plus vapour distillate flowrate (VT).
Reflux ratio normalized mol/mol or kg/kg Ratio of flowrate of liquid that is refluxed back (R) to the total vapour flow entering the condenser.
Top vapour product flowrate kmol/h or kg/s Flowrate of the vapour leaving the condenser as distillate.
Top liquid product flowrate kmol/h or kg/s Flowrate of the liquid leaving the condenser as distillate.
Bottom product flowrate kmol/h or kg/s Flowrate of the liquid product at the bottom of the column.
Top vapour product flowrate to feed flowrate ratio mol/mol or kg/kg Ratio of the flowrates of the vapour distillate to the feed.
Top liquid product flowrate to feed flowrate ratio mol/mol or kg/kg Ratio of the flowrates of the liquid distillate to the feed.
Bottom product flowrate to feed flowrate ratio mol/mol or kg/kg Ratio of the flowrates of the bottom to the feed.
Average column pressure bar Constant pressure being applied on the whole column.
Feed number - Index of the feed from which the pressure is specified.
Top column pressure bar

Specified pressure at the top of the column.
Bottom column pressure bar Specified pressure at the bottom of the column.
Pressure drop per stage bar Difference in pressure between two adjacent stages.
Liquid level in the condenser m

Used to determine the pressure head in the condenser.
Distillate pressure bar Pressure of the liquid stream leaving the condenser.
Pressure drop in the condenser bar Specified hydrostatic pressure drop in the condenser.
Liquid level in the reboiler m

Used to determine the pressure head in the reboiler.
Bottom product pressure bar Pressure of the liquid leaving the reboiler.
Pressure drop in the reboiler bar Specified hydrostatic pressure drop in the reboiler.
Non-condensables Selector Selects non-condensibles from the component list. These are assigned a composition of 0 in the liquid phase. It applies to the initial flash feed and the iterative initialisation procedure only. This option has no effect on the final results of the column model.
Non-volatiles Selector Selects non-volatiles from the component list. These are assigned a composition of 0 in the vapour phase. It applies to the initial flash feed and the iterative initialisation procedure only. This option has no effect on the final results of the column model.
