High shear granulator

The high shear granulator model is used to model a dynamic high shear wet granulation process.

Key features:

  • Creation of a wet granulate phase from fine powder particles by addition of liquid.
  • Evolution of granule liquid content due to drop nucleation and wetting mechanisms.
  • Granule growth via liquid-dependent layering and agglomeration.
  • Reduction in granule porosity due to consolidation.
