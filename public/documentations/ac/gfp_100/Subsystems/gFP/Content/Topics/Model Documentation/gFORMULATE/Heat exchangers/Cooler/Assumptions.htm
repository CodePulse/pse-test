Assumptions

The key assumptions made in this model are

  • Accumulation of material is not considered.
  • No particle transformation events (breakage, agglomeration, etc.) occur.
  • No chemical or nuclear reactions take place.
  • No phase change takes place due to heating of liquid or cooling of vapour streams.
  • Separate energy balances are adopted for each phase.
  • No heat exchange between phases takes place.
