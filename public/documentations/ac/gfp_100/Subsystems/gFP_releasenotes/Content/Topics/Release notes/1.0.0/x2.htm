Updating flowsheets from gCRYSTAL, gSOLIDS and gCOAS

The purpose of this section is to describe changes in functionality of the software (model libraries and platform functionality) and details for migrating legacy models when transitioning to gPROMS FormulatedProducts 1.0.0. The succeeding sections will indicate which models and platform features can be opened via legacy licenses in the new product. In addition, instructions for migration of legacy flowsheet models and cases are provided. This document is subject to revision as the procedures entailed within evolve. However, it is always the intention of PSE to maintain the functionality and usability of prior efforts.

If instructions in this guide fail to resolve your specific case please email PSE Formulated Products support (support.formulatedproducts@psenterprise.com) including any affected (flowsheet) models.
