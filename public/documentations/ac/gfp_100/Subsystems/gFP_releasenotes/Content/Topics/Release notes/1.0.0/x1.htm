Introduction to gPROMS FormulatedProducts

gPROMS FormulatedProducts 1.0 is PSE’s product for integrated modelling of active ingredients and formulated product manufacture, and subsequent product performance. It unifies the content and functionality of gCRYSTAL®, gSOLIDS® and gCOAS® to support end-to-end modelling of formulated product manufacture.

Enhancements to physical property modelling and database interaction, a new underlying model structure to enable the description of complex, composite phase formation of a product and many new unit operations are just a few of the new and improved functionality of the model libraries.
