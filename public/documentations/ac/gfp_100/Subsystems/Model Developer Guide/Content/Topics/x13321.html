Implicit Final Step

If, after the final NEXT section has been processed, one or more modifications of the Initialisation Procedure have not been REVERTed, a final implicit step will be performed where all remaining modifications are REVERTed to their original values in parallel.

Implicit final steps are always performed using a MOVE_TO.
