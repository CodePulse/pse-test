The Simple Process Modelling Environment

To help users and PSE support staff diagnose interoperability issues between gPROMS and other CAPE-OPEN compliant packages the Windows version of gPROMS comes with a command-line utility called SimplePME.exe.

  • Listing available CAPE-OPEN unit operations

  • Listing available CAPE-OPEN physical properties packages

  • Performing a calculation on a CAPE-OPEN unit operation
