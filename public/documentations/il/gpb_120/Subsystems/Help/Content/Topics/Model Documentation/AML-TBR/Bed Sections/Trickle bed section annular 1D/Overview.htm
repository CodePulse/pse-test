Trickle-bed section annular 1D

This model represents an annular section of a reactor tube filled with solid pellets. Gas and liquid trickle down the fixed-bed and the solid pellets are filled with liquid.

The fixed-bed model is axially distributed (1D) and considers separate conservation equations for fluid and catalyst (heterogeneous bed model). The pellets are represented either by a lumped model or by a distributed model considering the multi-component mass transfer inside catalyst pores.

The internal surface of the annular section will exchange heat with an inner tube model. Heat will also be exchanged through the tube external wall (axial 1D model) with a shell side model or another annular bed section model.

For more information on the empirical correlations, see 'Auxiliary Correlations'.
