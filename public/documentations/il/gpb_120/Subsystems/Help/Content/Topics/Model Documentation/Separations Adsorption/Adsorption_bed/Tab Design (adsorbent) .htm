Design (adsorbent)

The Design (adsorbent) tab is used to specify the types of adsorbent and the loading for each type of adsorbent, as well as the adsorbent pellet geometry.

Name Default Unit Description
Adsorbents per layer Selector
Single adsorbent per layer
Each layer uses a single type of adsorbent.
Multiple adsorbents per layer
Each layer uses multiple adsorbents. Properties of the adsorbent are entered per layer.
Amount of adsorbent specification Selector
Bed porosity and bed density
Use the bed porosity (also known as "bed void fraction") and bed bulk density to determine the amount of adsorbent in the bed and the extra-particle void fraction.
Mass of adsorbent and pellet bulk density
Use the mass of adsorbent in the bed and the pellet bulk density to determine the amount of adsorbent in the bed and the extra-particle void fraction.
Bed porosity and pellet bulk density
Use the bed porosity (also known as "bed void fraction") and the pellet bulk density to determine the amount of adsorbent in the bed and the extra-particle void fraction.
Bed density and pellet bulk density
Use the bed density and the pellet bulk density to determine the amount of adsorbent in the bed and the extra-particle void fraction.
