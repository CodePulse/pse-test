Main

The Main tab is used to specify data relating to the geometry and operation of the heat exchanger.

Name Default Unit Description
Design type Selector

Hairpin (1 unit = 2 double-pipes + 1 U-bend)

Selects a geometry where each of the units in series or parallel, specified in the dialog, consists of 2 straight double-pipes connected by one U-bend. The length used to calculate the heat exchange area is twice the length of a straight part plus the length of the calculated U-bend. The same length is used for pressure drop calculations for both fluids.
Straight (1 unit = 1 double-pipe)
Selects a geometry where each of the units in series or parallel, specified in the dialog, consists of a single straight double-pipe The length used to calculate the area for heat transfer is the length of the straight section of the double-pipe. Regarding pressure drop calculations, the length of the U-bend used by the inner fluid between straight sections is taken into account.
Flow arrangement Selector

Co-current

The flow of the inner fluid is in the same direction to the flow of the outer fluid.
Counter-current
The flow of the inner fluid is in the opposite direction to the flow of the outer fluid.
