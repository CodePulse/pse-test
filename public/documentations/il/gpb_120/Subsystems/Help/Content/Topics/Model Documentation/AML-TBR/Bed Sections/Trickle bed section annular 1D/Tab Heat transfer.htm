Heat transfer

The heat transfer mode and coefficients, wall geometry and properties are specified in the Heat transfer tab.

Name Default Unit Description
Axial conduction Selector
Off
Axial conduction is ignored.
On
Axial conduction is considered in wall and liquid phase only.
 
Heat transfer coefficient Selector
Overall
A single overall heat transfer coefficient (h.t.c) is used between the wall and the bed. The default correlations for effective bed conductivity and bed wall h.t.c. are used.
2D formulation (h.t.c. and conduction)
The overall heat transfer coefficient is obtained from the effective bed conductivity and the bed wall h.t.c..
