Common options

The Common options appear at the top of the dialog. Any changes to these options may affect all other tabs on the dialog.

Name Default Unit Description

Operation mode

Selector
Initialisation Procedure (start from zero flowrate)
An initial outlet flowrate of zero and a specified outlet pressure are used to help initialise the model.
Initialisation Procedure
An initial outlet flowrate, initial outlet pressure, initial outlet composition and an initial outlet temperature are used to help initialise the model.
Closed
The inlet and outlet streams are identical and no initialisation procedure is used. This option may be selected to initialise dynamic flowsheets or steady-state flowsheets using Saved Variable Sets (SVS).
Open
The model outlets are specified instead of being equal to the inlets. This option may be selected to diagnose initialisation issues in a given flowsheet.
Component specification Selector
Component fractions and overall flowrate
The stream composition and flowrate is specified in terms of the component fractions and the overall flowrate.
Component flowrates
The stream composition and flowrate is specified in terms of the individual component flowrates.
Composition specification Selector
Mass
Mass basis is selected for composition specification.
Molar
Molar basis is selected for composition specification.
Flowrate specification Selector
Mass
Mass basis is selected for flowrate specification.
Molar
Molar basis is selected for flowrate specification.
