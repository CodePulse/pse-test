Heat exchanger

This model represents a heat exchanger in which heat is exchanged between two material streams. The desired outlet temperature of one of the streams can be specified directly, and then the outlet temperature of the other stream will be calculated. Alternatively, the heat exchange area and the heat transfer coefficient between the fluid streams can be specified. In that case both outlet temperatures will be predicted. For sizing and prediction, two methods are implemented: the log-mean temperature difference method and the effectiveness-NTU method.
