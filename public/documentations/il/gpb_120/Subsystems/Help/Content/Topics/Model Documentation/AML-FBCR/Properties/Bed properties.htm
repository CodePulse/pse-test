Bed properties

The bed properties models contain equations for calculation of equivalent diameters, bed void fraction, specific surface area of the pellets and various correlations for pressure drop, heat transfer and mass transfer. The correlations in each of the models are described below. For more information about the specific correlations used in these models, see 'Correlations'.

Bed properties for 1D bed models (bed_properties_1D):

  • Bed void fraction

  • Pressure drop equation and correlation for Ergun-type equation parameters

  • Overall heat transfer coefficient

  • Effective bed thermal conductivity

  • Bed-wall heat transfer coefficient

  • Pellet-gas mass transfer coefficient and heat transfer coefficient

Bed properties for 2D bed models (bed_properties_2D):

  • Bed void fraction

  • Pressure drop equation and correlation for Ergun-type equation parameters

  • Effective bed thermal conductivity

  • Bed-wall heat transfer coefficient

  • Pellet-gas mass transfer coefficient and heat transfer coefficient

Bed properties for 2D radial-flow bed models (bed_properties_2D_in_radial_flow_bed):

  • Bed void fraction

  • Effective bed thermal conductivity

  • Pellet-gas mass transfer coefficient and heat transfer coefficient

Bed properties for 2D radial-flow bed models (bed_properties_2D_in_radial_flow_bed_with_cooling):

  • Bed void fraction

  • Effective bed thermal conductivity

  • Bed-wall heat transfer coefficient

  • Pellet-gas mass transfer coefficient and heat transfer coefficient
