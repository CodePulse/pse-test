General settings

The inert section design and bed properties are specified in the General settings tab.

Name Default Unit Description
Bed porosity calculation Selector
Specified
Bed porosity is directly specified by the user.
Mass of catalyst
Bed porosity is calculated from a mass of catalyst specification.
Haughey and Beveridge
Bed porosity is predicted from a predefined correlation.
Custom
Bed porosity is predicted from a user-defined correlation.
Reactor geometry
Inert section length m Length of the bed section.
Bed properties
Inert mass per tube kg Mass of inert pellets in the tube section, as defined in the bed properties model.
Bed porosity m3/m3 Void fraction of the bed. Defined as the ratio of the free volume between pellets and the total bed volume.
Bed porosity adjustable factor - Parameter used for bed porosity user defined calculation.
