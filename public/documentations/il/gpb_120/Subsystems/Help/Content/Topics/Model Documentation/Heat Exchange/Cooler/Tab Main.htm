The Main tab is used to specify data relating to the thermal operation of the cooler.

Enthalpy
Saturated liquid (Vapour fraction = 0)
When selecting this option, the outlet stream will initially consist of a saturated liquid (i.e. the outlet stream temperature is the bubble temperature of the mixture).
Saturated vapour (Vapour fraction = 1)
When selecting this option, the outlet stream will initially consist of a saturated vapour (i.e. the outlet stream temperature is the dew temperature of the mixture).
Duty specification Selector
Specified here
Use the value of the heat duty provided in this dialog.
From energy connection
The heat duty exchanged in the unit is obtained from the inlet energy connection. The thermodynamic sign convention is used (e.g. if heat is to be removed from the system then the duty specification will be negative).
