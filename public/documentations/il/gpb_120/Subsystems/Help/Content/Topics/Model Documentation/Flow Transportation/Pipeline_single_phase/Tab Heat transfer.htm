Heat transfer

The Heat transfer tab is used to specify data relative to the heat transfer between the pipe fluid and the surroundings.

Name Default Unit Description
Pipe fluid to wall heat transfer coefficient Selector
Specify
Specifies the heat transfer coefficient from pipe fluid to wall directly.
Calculate
Calculates the heat transfer coefficient from pipe fluid to wall from a given correlation.
Wall to surroundings heat transfer coefficient Selector
Specify
Specifies the heat transfer coefficient from the wall to the surroundings directly.
Calculate
Calculates the heat transfer coefficient from the wall to the surroundings from a given correlation.
Pipe fluid to wall heat transfer coefficient correlation Selector
Power-law
Calculates the heat transfer coefficient from a power-law correlation.
Dittus-Boelter
Calculates the heat transfer coefficient from the Dittus-Boelter correlation (Shah & Sekulic, 2003, p. 482)1Shah, R.K. & Sekulic, D.P., 2003. Fundamentals of Heat Exchanger Design, John Wiley & Sons, Inc.
Gnielinski
Calculates the heat transfer coefficient from the Gnielinski correlation (VDI Heat Atlas, 2010)2VDI-Gesellschaft Verfahrenstechnik und Chemieingenieurwesen, 2010. Heat Atlas, 2nd Ed., Springer..
Custom
User-supplied equations listed in the htc_channel_1D_custom_gML model are given to determine the heat transfer coefficient.
