The correlation from Fair has been the standard of the industry for entrainment flood prediction. It may be used tor sieve, valve, or bubble-cap trays. The application of the correlation is subject to the following restrictions:

1. System is low or nonfoaming.

2. Weir height is less than 15 percent of tray spacing.

3. Sieve-tray perforations are 13 mm or less in diameter.

4. Ratio of slot (bubble cap), perforation (sieve), or full valve opening (valve plate) area to active area is 0.1 or greater.
