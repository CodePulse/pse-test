Air diffusers

The Air diffusers tab is used to specify the characteristics of the air diffusion system, which may affect the Kla calculation and the pressure drop.

Name Default Unit Description

Properties as inputs
The parameters, used to compute the head losses through the air diffusers, are specified by the user.
