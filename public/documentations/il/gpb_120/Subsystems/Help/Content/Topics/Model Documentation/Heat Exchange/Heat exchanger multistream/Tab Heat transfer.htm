Heat transfer

The Heat transfer tab is used to specify heat transfer coefficients

Name Default Unit Description
Heat transfer coefficient specification Selector
Specified overall heat transfer coefficient (U) per connection
One overall heat transfer coefficient, U, is specified for each connection between a pair of channels. This is used to calculate the individual heat transfer coefficients on each side of the wall, assuming the resistance to heat transfer on each side to be equal.
Specified single heat transfer coefficient (k) per channel
Specify the heat transfer coefficient, k, for each channel.
Calculated single heat transfer coefficient (k) per channel
The heat transfer coefficient, k, for each channel is determined from a correlation that relates the fluid conditions and properties inside of the channel to the heat transfer from fluid to channel wall.
Heat transfer coefficient correlation Selector
Custom
User-supplied equations listed in the htc_channel_1D_custom_gML model are given to determine the heat transfer coefficient.

Heat transfer coefficient adjustable factor - Factor that can be used to adjust predictions from heat transfer correlations to match observed results.
