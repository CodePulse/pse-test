Description

Two examples are available that illustrate the application of the AML:FBCR to two different processes of natural gas reforming. These are:

  1. Steam methane reforming (SMR)

    This case study is based on previous published work on methane steam reforming1J. Xu, G.F. Froment, Methane Steam Reforming : ll. Diffusional Limitations and Reactor Simulation, 35 (1989) 97–103. , 2P.M. Plehiers, G.F. Froment, Coupled Simulation of Heat Transfer and Reaction in a Steam Reforming Furnace, 12 (1989) 20–26.. The AML:FBCR is used to model the catalyst tube side in detail and uses simplified models for the furnace side.

    This example contains the following project files:

    • AML-FBCR D03 - Kinetics and Properties
    • AML-FBCR D03a - Natural gas reforming - SMR

  2. Autothermal reforming (ATR)

    This case study is based on published work on reforming in fertilizer plants3A. AL-Dhfeery, A. Jassem, Modeling and simulation of an industrial secondary reformer reactor in the fertilizer plants, Int. J. Ind. Chem. 3 (2012) 14.. The catalyst bed is modelled using the AML:FBCR while the combustion chamber is modelled with a simplified conversion reactor.

    This example contains the following project files:

    • AML-FBCR D03 - Kinetics and Properties
    • AML-FBCR D03b - Natural gas reforming - ATR

Note the "Kinetics and Properties" project needs to be loaded in all examples as it contains information on the kinetics and the physical properties of the system.
