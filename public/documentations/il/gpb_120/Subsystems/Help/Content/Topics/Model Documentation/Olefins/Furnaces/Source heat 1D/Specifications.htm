Specifications

The Source heat 1D model comprises the following specifications.

Name Default Unit Description
Specifications Selector
Off
Turns off the unit dialog.
On
Turns on the unit dialog.

Mode

 Selector
Adiabatic
Zero heat flux to cracking tube.
Constant heat flux
Allows for the specification a constant heat flux to the cracking tube.
Heat flux profile predicted
The heat flux profile is calculated using the net radiant heat transfer equation.
Heat flux profile uniform
A constant heat flux is calculated.
Heat flux profile (Lookup table)
The heat flux profile to cracking tube is calculated using a relative heat flux provided via a data file.
Cooling jacket
Allows to simulate heat transfer to a cooling medium and requires the specification of heat transfer coefficient and coolant temperature.

Flow direction in the tube

 Selector
Up
The process fluid in the tube connected to the unit flows in the upward direction.
Down
The process fluid in the tube connected to the unit flows in the downward direction.
Heat flux kW/m2 Heat flux to the cracking tube .
Emissivity - Emissivity used in the net radiant heat transfer equation.
Temperature (Flame) K Flame temperature used in the net radiant heat transfer equation.
Coolant temperature K Temperature of the tube coolant.
Coolant h.t.c. W m-2 K-1 Heat transfer coefficient for the coolant side of the tube.
Relative heat flux profile - The data file containing the relative heat flux profile. The relative heat flux profile should be expressed as the ratio of heat flux at each axial position to the maximum heat flux.
