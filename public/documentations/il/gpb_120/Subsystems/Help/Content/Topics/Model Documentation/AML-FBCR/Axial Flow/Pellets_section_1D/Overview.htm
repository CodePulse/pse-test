Catalyst pellets section 1D

This model represents a section of a reactor tube filled with solid pellets. Gas or liquid flow through the fixed-bed.

The fixed-bed model is axially distributed (1D) and considers separate conservation equations for fluid and catalyst (heterogeneous bed model). The pellets are represented either by a lumped model or by a distributed model considering the multi-component mass transfer inside catalyst pores.

The reactor section can either be adiabatic or heat transfer through the tube wall can be considered. When heat transfer is considered, the heat is exchanged through the tube wall (axial 1D model). The model must be connected to a shell side model or an annular section model via the FBRDistributedThermalContact port.

For more information on the empirical correlations, see 'Correlations'.
