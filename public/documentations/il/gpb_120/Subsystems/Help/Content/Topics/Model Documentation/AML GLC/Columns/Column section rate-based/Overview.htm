Column section rate-based

This model describes a packed column with liquid and gas phases in countercurrent continuous contact. Mass and heat transfer between the phases takes place at finite rates via liquid and vapour films separated by an interface. Phase equilibrium conditions hold at the interface only. The model predicts mass transfer between the phases, pressure drop in the column and liquid and vapour holdup in the column.

The section column does not have a reboiler or a condenser and therefore there is no need to provide specifications in the operation tab (e.g. Reflux ratio or Bottom flowrate). The other specifications of this model are similar to those of the distillation column.
