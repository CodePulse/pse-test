Specifications

The Reactor stirred tank VLE model specification dialog comprises the following tabs:

Name

Description
Design Specifications related to the unit design.
Operation

Parameters related to operational aspects.
Dynamics Specifications related to dynamic simulation.
Heat transfer Parameters related to the heat transfer in the unit.
Reactions Phases for the reactions.
Reactions (Liquid) Liquid reactions specifications.

Reactions (Liquid) - rate expression

 Parameters related to the chosen rate expression in the liquid phase.
Reactions (Liquid) - kinetic constants Parameters related to the kinetic constants in the liquid phase.
Reactions (Liquid) - equilibrium constants Parameters related to the equilibrium constants in the liquid phase.
Reactions (Vapour) Vapour reactions specifications.

Reactions (Vapour) - rate expression

 Parameters related to the chosen rate expression in the vapour phase.
Reactions (Vapour) - kinetic constants Parameters related to the kinetic constants in the vapour phase.
Reactions (Vapour) - equilibrium constants Parameters related to the equilibrium constants in the vapour phase.
Design (Wall) Parameters related to the shape and dimensions of the wall of the reactor.
Heat transfer (Wall) Parameters related to the heat transfer to the surroundings.
Initial guesses Initial guesses used to initialise the unit model and the flowsheet.

Numerics

 Specifications related to numerical parameters.
