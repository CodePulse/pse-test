Design (Wall)

The Design (Wall) tab is used to specify data related to the shape and dimensions of the wall of the reactor.

Name Default Unit Description
Shape Selector
Hollow cylinder
Selects cylinder shape for the wall.
Hollow abstract shape
Selects an abstract shape for the wall. Volume and area of the abstract shape must be provided.

Outer radius specification

 Selector
Wall thickness
Determines the outer radius from the wall thickness.
Outer diameter
Determines the outer radius from its diameter.
Outer radius
Specifies the outer radius directly.
Outer area
Determines the outer radius by specifying the outer area of the wall.
Cylinder length Selector
Length
Specifies the vessel length directly.
Volume
Determines the length from the volume specification.
