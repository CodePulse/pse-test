The valve time constant τ is measured from the first detectable response of the system (in response to a small step) until the system reaches 63% of its final steady-state-value.

Dead time can be accounted for by specifying the time constant T63 as the time constant τ. The time constant T63 is measured from the time the step input is initiated to the time when the system output reaches 63% of the final steady-state value. T63 is therefore a combination of the valve dead time and the valve time constant.
