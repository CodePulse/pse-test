Heat transfer

The Heat transfer tab is used to set the correlations and parameters used to calculate the heat transfer in the cracking tube.

Name Default Unit Description
Empirical h.t.c. adjustment -

Tuning factor to match the observed tube metal temperature profile at certain conditions.
Coke conductivity at 1073 K W m-1 K-1

Thermal conductivity of coke at 1073 K.
Coke conductivity T-dependence parameter W m-1 K-2

Temperature dependence parameter for calculating the coke thermal conductivity.

Coke thermal conductivity = Coke conductivity at 1073 K + Coke conductivity T-dependence parameter * (T - 1073).
Tube wall conductivity at 1073 K W m-1 K-1 The tube wall conductivity at 1073 K.
Tube wall conductivity T-dependence parameter W m-1 K-2

Temperature dependence parameter for calculating the wall thermal conductivity.

Tube wall thermal conductivity = Tube wall conductivity at 1073 K + Tube wall conductivity T-dependence parameter * (T - 1073).
