Monitoring the activity progress

You can monitor the progress that is being made during any Parameter Estimation activity from the Execution Output window. From the Execution Output window you can expand or collapse portions of the output by clicking on the + or - signs in the tree associated with the executing activity.

Monitoring the progress of a Parameter Estimation activity.

Adjusting the output level in the SOLUTIONPARAMETERS section of the Process will enable you to change the amount of information sent to the execution window during the execution of an Estimation activity.
