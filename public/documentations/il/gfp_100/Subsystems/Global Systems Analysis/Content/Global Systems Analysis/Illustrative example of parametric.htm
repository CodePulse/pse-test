Illustrative example of a parametric case study

In this example, GSA is used to perform a parametric analysis to determine how the inlet mole fraction affects the yield and temperature in a CSTR reactor.

There is one factor, the inlet molar fraction, and two responses, yield and temperature. The yield is plotted against the inlet molar fraction, showing a non-linear effect of the inlet molar fraction on the yield.

A high level description of the case study can be found in 'Description of the illustrative example system'. Instructions to reproduce these results can be found in 'Case 7: Model level parametric case study'.
