Dosage form material

The gPROMS name of the dosage form connection type is Dosage_form_material_gFP.

The dosage form material connection type is based on the Material connection type, with the inclusion of several dosage form related parameters and variables. The extra parameters and variables are shown in the top section. The full list is shown in the second section.
