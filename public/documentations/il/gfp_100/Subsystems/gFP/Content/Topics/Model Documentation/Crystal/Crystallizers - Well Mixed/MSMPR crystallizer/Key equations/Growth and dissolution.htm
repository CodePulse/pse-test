Crystal growth and dissolution

Five model options are available in the Crystallizer model for growth and dissolution rate expressions:

  1. Inactive
  2. Two step kinetics (Mersmann)
  3. Two step kinetics (Classical)
  4. Power law kinetics
  5. Custom kinetics
