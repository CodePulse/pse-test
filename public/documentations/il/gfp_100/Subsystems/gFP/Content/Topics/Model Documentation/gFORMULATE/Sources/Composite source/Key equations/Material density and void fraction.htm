Mass densities and void fractions of particles

Inter-particle void is the void between particles. Intra-particle void is the internal void with in particles. Bulk mass density is the mass per particle bulk volume, including inter-particle void and intra-particle void. Particle mass density is the mass per particle volume including only intra-particle volume. The relationship between the parameters are shown below.
