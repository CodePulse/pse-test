Assumptions

The following assumptions are made in deriving the model of the spray dryer.

  • The vapour phase is assumed to be perfectly mixed.
  • The droplet phase is assumed to follow plug flow (i.e. no back-mixing of droplets is assumed to take place)
  • Agglomeration or breakage of droplets is not considered.
  • Heat transfer between droplets and vapour is considered. Additional heat losses are not calculated but can be specified by the user.
