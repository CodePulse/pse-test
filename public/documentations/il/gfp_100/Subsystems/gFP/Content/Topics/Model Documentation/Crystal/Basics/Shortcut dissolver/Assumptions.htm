Assumptions

The model is developed under the following assumptions:

  • The model has no hold-up.
  • For cases where dissolution is incomplete (or where crystallisation occurs), the particle size distribution is scaled by the ratio of outlet to inlet crystal mass. Note: in those cases the shape of the PSD does not change.
