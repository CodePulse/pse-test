Equipment correlations

Five model options are available in the Wet mill model for correlations between the breakage rate and the mill equipment:

  1. None
  2. Shear rate
  3. Shear rate - Karabelas et al. (1976)
  4. Shear frequency
  5. Shear number

The expressions suggested by Karabelas et al. (1976), shear frequency and shear number equipment correlations options are described in the following sections.

Note: In the equations below, parameters to be specified or estimated are printed in blue.

Application to the breakage kernels

The scale-up/equipment correlations outlined above have been applied to the breakage rate constant terms (ScM or c in the case of the de Vegt (2005) breakage kernel). For cases, where the equipment correlation is set to “None”, these parameters can be specified as shown in section 3. However, when any of the equipment correlations above are employed the breakage rate constant terms are changed from being assigned to being calculated, dependent upon the C and C0 equipment correlation fitting parameters.
