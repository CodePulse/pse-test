Lack of fit test

The lack of fit test table, as shown below, provides a comparison of the weighted residual and chi-squared value (χ2 – Value (95%)). A weighted residual less than the χ2–Value (95%) indicates a good fit between the model and the measured data. It also indicates whether the model sufficiently describes the behaviour data. For cases where the weighted residual is greater than the χ2–Value (95%), the model may need to be modified to describe the experimental data.
