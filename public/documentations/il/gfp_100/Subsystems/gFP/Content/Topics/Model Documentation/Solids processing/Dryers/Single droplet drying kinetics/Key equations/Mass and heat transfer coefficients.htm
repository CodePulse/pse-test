Mass and heat transfer coefficients

Three options are provided for the determination of mass and heat transfer coefficients.

Externally limited regime

In this scenario, it is assumed that the drying rate is limited by diffusion of liquid from the surface of the solid particle to the ambient environment. Two options are available for the mass transfer correlations. These are described below.
