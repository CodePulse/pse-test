Weibull/Rosin Rammler distribution

A Weibull/Rosin Rammler distribution is a continuous probability distribution and was first applied by Rosin & Rammler 1Rosin, P. and Rammler, E., The laws governing the fineness of powder coal, Journal of the Institute of Fuel, 7 (1933), 29-36. to describe the particle size distributions of powdered coal.
