Horizontal blender

The horizontal blender model describes the transport of material through a continuous Horizontal blender via a plug flow description including convective and dispersive terms. Additionally, adhesion of material to surfaces may be considered, driven by a concentration gradient.

Key features:

  • Plug flow description of transport of material through the mixer.
  • Dispersive transport is considered.
  • Adhesion of material to the unit surfaces may also be considered.
