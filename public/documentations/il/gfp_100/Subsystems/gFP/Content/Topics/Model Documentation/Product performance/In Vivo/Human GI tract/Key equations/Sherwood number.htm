Sherwood number

The Sherwood number represents the ratio of the convective mass transfer to the rate of diffusive mass transport, and is assumed to be 2 in the in vivo models. In the in vitro models, the Sherwood number may be calculated based on the impellor diameter, stirrer speed and impellor power number according to a correlation applicable to stirred vessels (Garside et al.
