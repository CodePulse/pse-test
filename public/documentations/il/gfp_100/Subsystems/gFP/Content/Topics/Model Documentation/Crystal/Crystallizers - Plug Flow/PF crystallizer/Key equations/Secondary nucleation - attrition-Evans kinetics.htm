Evans kinetics

A model based upon the work of Evans et al. 1 Evans, T.W., Sarofim, A.F. and Margolis, G. (1974). Models of Secondary Nucleation attributable to Crystal-Crystallizer and Crystal-Crystal Collisions, AIChE Journal, 20(5), 959. can be used to describe secondary nucleation resulting from attrition. Three options are available for implementing this model, as follows:

  1. Crystal – impeller (Default and original model) – used for STR’s where secondary nucleation by means of attrition is mainly caused by collision between the crystal and the impeller.
  2. Crystal – crystal – only accounts for attrition caused by crystal – crystal collisions.
  3. Both – consider attrition caused by both crystal – crystal and crystal – impeller collisions.
