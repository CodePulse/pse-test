The gSAFT physical properties interface

PSE’s SAFT technology is a general and efficient implementation of the SAFT-VR SW and SAFT-γ Mie equation of states and is available as the gSAFT Physical Properties Package. For more background please refer to the PSE gSAFT web site and the publications below.

SAFT-γ Mie
Lafitte T, Apostolakou A, Avendaño C, Galindo A, Adjiman CS, Müller, EA, and Jackson G, “Accurate statistical associating fluid theory for chains formed from Mie segments, Journal of Chemical Physics, 139, 154504 (2013).
Papaioannou V, Lafitte T, Avendaño C, Adjiman CS, Jackson G, Müller, EA, and Galindo A, “Group contribution methodology based on the statistical associating fluid theory for heteronuclear molecular models formed from Mie segments”. Journal of Chemical Physics, 140, 054107 (2014).
SAFT-VT SW
Gil-Villegas, A. Galindo, P.J. Whitehead, S. J. Mills, G. Jackson, and A. N. Burgess, “Statistical association fluid theory for chain molecules with attractive potentials of variable range (SAFT-VR)”, Journal of Chemical Physics, 106, 4168-4186 (1997).
A. Galindo, L. A. Davies, A. Gil-Villegas, and G. Jackson, “The Thermodynamics of mixtures and the corresponding mixing rules in the SAFT-VR approach for potentials of variable range”, Molecular Physics, 93, 241-252 (1998).

In order to use gSAFT in gPROMS models, there are three basic steps that need to be followed:

  1. Choose the equation of state:

    • SAFT-VR SW or
    • SAFT-γ Mie.

  2. Configure the gSAFT interface with the following information, the

    • name of the databank to use and the
    • list of components of interest – note that the available components are different for SAFT-VR SW and SAFT-γ Mie.

  3. Call one or more of the methods provided by the gSAFT interface. At present the gSAFT interface supports a sub-set of the standard set and it is important to note that gSAFT can be used in parallel with another physical property package such as Multiflash. Multiflash could be used to provide transport properties for the same mixture.
