Topology connections and the gPROMS language

Each Unit on the Topology corresponds to a declaration of a Model instance. The Unit declarations are shown (and can be edited) on the gPROMS language tab in the UNIT section.

Each connection on the Topology corresponds to an equation that equates the two Ports1More explicitly, gPROMS expands the Port definition to equate all the variables in the two Ports.. Like the Unit declarations, the equations are shown (and can be edited) on the gPROMS language tab in the TOPOLOGY section.
