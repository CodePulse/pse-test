Heat recovery steam generator

The Heat recovery steam generator (or HRSG) model can be used to represent steam generation by heat recovery from hot exhaust gases from a gas turbine. The HRSG has inlet and outlet streams for gas and water / steam. The model is configured by specifying the status, steam temperature and pressure and either the gas outlet temperature or the steam production curve.
