Workspaces and ghost projects

The files belonging to a workspace can be manipulated outside of gPROMS ModelBuilder, for instance by renaming, moving or deleting them. In this case, gPROMS ModelBuilder cannot track the change and update the workspace accordingly. When gPROMS ModelBuilder switches to such a workspace, some projects may not be available under their last known location and name and they are called Ghost Projects.

Ghost projects appear at the top of the project tree and are displayed in grey.

Ghost projects in the project tree

When hovering with the mouse cursor over such a project, a tooltip denotes the location under which it was saved in the workspace.

Tooltip with location

gPROMS ModelBuilder, in a context menu, gives the options to close the project or to try re-opening it after the project file has been restored in its location.
